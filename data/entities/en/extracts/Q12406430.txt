Hed Arzi Music (Hebrew: הד ארצי‎, meaning: The Echo of My Country / My Country's Echo; also spelled Hed Artzi or Hed Artsi), is an Israeli record label founded by Zvi Levin in 1946. Hed Arzi is involved with producing music, importing albums from abroad, and distributing Israeli and international music. Hed Arzi is one of the largest and most veteran companies in Israel, and many major artists work with it.
Besides its involvement in music, the company also imports and distributes multimedia products, such as computer games and videos.
