Kičevo (Macedonian: Кичево [ˈkitʃɛvɔ] (listen); Albanian: Kërçovë; Turkish: Kırçova) is a city in the western part of the Republic of North Macedonia, located in a valley in the south-eastern slopes of Mount Bistra, between the cities of Ohrid and Gostivar. The capital Skopje is 112 km away. The city of Kičevo is the seat of Kičevo Municipality.
