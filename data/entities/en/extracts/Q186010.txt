Richard Luigi Di Natale (born 6 June 1970) is an Australian politician who serves as a senator for Victoria. He was also the leader of the Australian Greens from 2015 to 2020. Di Natale was elected to the Australian Senate in the 2010 federal election. A former general practitioner, Di Natale became federal parliamentary leader of the Australian Greens on 6 May 2015 following the resignation of Christine Milne and led the Greens during the 2016 and 2019 federal elections.
