Lidia Ravera (born 6 February 1951 in Turin, Piedmont) is an Italian writer, journalist, essayist and screenwriter.Ravera has been a regular contributor to the italian edition of Cosmopolitan. Her most popular novel, Porci con le ali ("Winged Pigs"), dealt with the disillusionment of her generation with the ideals of the late 1960s. In 1977 Ravera wrote a film adaptation of the book.
