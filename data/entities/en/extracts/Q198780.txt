Bertrand of Orléans-Braganza (born 2 February 1941, Mandelieu-la-Napoule, Vichy France) is a member of the Imperial House of Brazil. According to the disputed claims of the Vassouras branch of the family, he is first in the line of succession to the defunct Brazilian throne, and as such is the current claimant of the title of Prince Imperial of Brazil. Bertrand is also related to the House of Braganza and the House of Orléans (Orleanist claimants), both by his father's lineage, and to the House of Wittelsbach by his mother's lineage.
