SPV GmbH (also known as Schallplatten Produktion und Vertrieb GmbH) is an independent German record label.
Founded on 1 January 1984, it has slowly grown to be one of the largest independent distributors and record labels worldwide.It also has several sub-labels that it produces and distributes, including the labels Steamhammer (Heavy Metal, Hard rock), Long Branch Records (Alternative, Indie, Progressive Rock, Progressive Metal, Metalcore), Oblivion (Darkwave, Gothic), SPV Recordings (Pop, Rock) and Cash Machine Records (Hip Hop).
