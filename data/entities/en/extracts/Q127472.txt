Slot (Russian: Слот) is a Russian metal band. The members of the group: Daria "Nookie" Stavrovich, Igor "Cache" Lobanov, Sergey "ID" Bogolyubsky, Nikita Muravyov, Vasiliy «Ghost» Gorshkov.
Since its inception, the group has released 8 studio albums, 2 remix albums, 2 mini-albums, 2 live DVDs, recorded 20 singles and several soundtracks. The slot is known for its long tours of Russia and neighboring countries, which sometimes last more than a year.
