Richard Bruce Kind (born November 22, 1956) is an American actor and voice actor, known for his roles in the sitcoms Mad About You (as Dr. Mark Devanow),  Spin City (as Paul Lassiter), and Curb Your Enthusiasm (as Larry's cousin, Andy) He also does voice performances in various Pixar films such as A Bug's Life, the Cars series, Toy Story 3, and Inside Out.
