Kira Buckland (born July 16, 1987) is an American voice actress who has provided voices for English dubbed Japanese anime and video games. Some of her major roles are 2B in Nier: Automata, Reimi Sugimoto in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Diamond Is Unbreakable, Hiyoko Saionji and Kirumi Tojo in the Danganronpa series, Talim and 2B in Soulcalibur VI, Heart Aino in BlazBlue: Cross Tag Battle, Falke in Street Fighter V, Edna in Tales of Zestiria, Mary Saotome in Kakegurui, and Julis-Alexia von Riessfeld in The Asterisk War.
