Ricardo Emmanuel Brown (born November 23, 1972), of the stage name Kurupt, is an American rapper who entered the public eye with Death Row Records in the early 1990s, especially once teaming with labelmate Daz to form a rap duo, Tha Dogg Pound. Kurupt left the label in 1996, and his debut solo album arrived in 1998. Since then Kurupt has been a member of other rap supergroups, such as the DPGC, which includes Snoop Dogg, and the Diirty OGz. Kurupt's rap talent has influenced standout rappers Eminem and Kendrick Lamar.
