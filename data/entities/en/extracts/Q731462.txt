Xavier Huillard (born 27 June 1954) is a French business executive and the current CEO of Vinci SA. He has held the position since 2006, he also served as chairman of the board since 2010. He has been employed with Vinci since March 1998, as Deputy General Manager, Chairman of Vinci Construction, and Chairman of Vinci Energies. Prior to joining Vinci, he was chairman and CEO of Sogea. He has been the chairman of the Institut de l’Entreprise since 2011. He graduated from École Polytechnique and École des ponts ParisTech.
