Andrea Wolf (Kurdish nickname: Ronahî, born January 15, 1965 in Munich, died October 23, 1998 in Çatak, Van province, Turkey) was a radical leftist activist. She was a PKK militant. She was killed while fighting with the PKK in 1998. Before her death, Wolf had been implicated in some revolutionary activities, and was accused of being part of the Red Army Faction from Germany. The Andrea Wolf case is the focus of a short film by Hito Steyerl called November.
