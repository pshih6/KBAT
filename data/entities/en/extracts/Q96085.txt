Monica Christiane Lierhaus (born 25 May 1970 in Hamburg) is a German sports journalist.In 1989 she passed her Abitur at the Charlotte-Paulsen-Gymnasium in Hamburg. She studied English and German literature at the University of Hamburg during which she worked as a junior in the editorial office of the magazine Sport-Bild. She also worked as a freelancer for German radio stations Radio Hamburg and Klassik Radio. In 1992 she dropped out of university to become an anchorwoman for the Hamburg local news show on Sat.1.
Between 1994 and 1996 she was a reporter for Sat.1 working in Germany and abroad.
