Anna-Katharina Samsel (born 12 April 1985 in Hanover) is a German actress.
Samsel grew up in Wolfsburg where she completed her Abitur. In 1999 she became the German artistic roller skating champion and won the World Championships in 1999, 2000 and 2002.Samsel appeared in the music video of Coldplay's 2008 single "Lost!".
