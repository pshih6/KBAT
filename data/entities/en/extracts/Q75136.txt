Silvia Seidel (September 23, 1969 - July 31, 2012) was a German actress from Munich, who starred in the TV series Anna (1987), Halfway Across the Galaxy and Turn Left (1993), and Sturm der Liebe (2007). She also starred in the films Anna – Der Film (1988), Faith (1990), and had a small role in The NeverEnding Story (1984). She died on July 31, 2012.She took her own life after suffering from depression. 
Her mother had also taken her own life in 1992.
She is buried in the Grünwald Woodland Cemetery in Munich.
