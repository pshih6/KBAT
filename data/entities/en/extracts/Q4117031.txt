Ameera bint Aidan bin Nayef Al-Taweel Al-Otaibi (Arabic: الأميرة أميرة بنت عيدان بن نايف الطويل العصيمي العتيبي‎; born 6 November 1983) is a Saudi former princess and philanthropist. Born into a non-royal cadet branch of the Saud dynasty, Ameera married her distant cousin Prince Al-Waleed bin Talal al Saud, and assumed the role of Vice Chairperson of Al-Waleed bin Talal Foundation. Al-Taweel is a member of the board of trustees at Silatech and is currently married to Emirati billionaire Khalifa bin Butti al Muhairi.
