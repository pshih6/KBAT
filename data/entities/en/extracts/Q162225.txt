Fallen is a 1998 American supernatural detective thriller film directed by Gregory Hoblit, produced by Charles Roven and Dawn Steel, from a screenplay by Nicholas Kazan. The film tells the story of John Hobbes, a Philadelphia police detective who is investigating murders committed by an apparent copycat killer. Denzel Washington, John Goodman, Donald Sutherland, Embeth Davidtz, James Gandolfini and Elias Koteas star.  Fallen was released on January 16, 1998, by Warner Bros. The film grossed $25.2 million against its budget of $46 million.  It has a 40% approval rating at Rotten Tomatoes, which calls it "not all that thrilling".
