Gregor Meyle (born October 13, 1978) is a German singer-songwriter. He first rose to prominence when he auditioned for the talent contest SSDSDSSWEMUGABRTLAD, hosted in Stefan Raab's late-night-show TV total in late-2007, eventually finishing in second place behind Stefanie Heinzmann.

Meyle achieved even more public recognition when he participated in the reality television series Sing meinen Song - Das Tauschkonzert in 2014, the German version of The Best Singers series.
