Richard Paull Goldin (born January 5, 1965) is an American actor, producer, director and television personality. He is known for his roles in daytime drama as Jake Martin in ABC's All My Children.  In May 2013, Goldin joined the cast of the CBS's The Bold and the Beautiful.  Goldin also hosts the HGTV series Spontaneous Construction, which premiered on the network on February 15, 2013.
