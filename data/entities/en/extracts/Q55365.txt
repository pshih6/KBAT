Cezary Pazura (born 13 June 1962 in Tomaszów Mazowiecki) is a popular Polish actor known for his comedy roles in movies such as Kiler, Chłopaki nie płaczą, Kariera Nikosia Dyzmy and a sitcom 13 posterunek. 
In addition to his work as a movie actor, Pazura is also a stand-up comedian and a popular voice actor. He provided voice for movies dubbed in Polish including Lady and the Tramp (as the Tramp), Ice Age (as Sid), Astérix & Obélix: Mission Cléopâtre (as Nubernaibs), Shark Tale (as Oscar) and for Pink Panther in various computer games. His brother Radosław Pazura is also an actor.
