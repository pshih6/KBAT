Svitavy (Czech pronunciation: [ˈsvɪtavɪ]; German: Zwittau) is a town in the Pardubice Region of the Czech Republic. The town has a population of 17,000 and is also the district administrative centre. It is the birthplace of Oskar Schindler and an important centre of the Czech Esperanto movement, with an Esperanto museum that is part of the city museum.
