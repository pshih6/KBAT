Charles Matthew Hunnam (; born 10 April 1980) is an English actor. He is known for his roles as Nathan Maloney in the Channel 4 drama Queer as Folk (1999–2000), Lloyd Haythe in the Fox comedy series Undeclared (2001–2002), the title role in Nicholas Nickleby (2002), Pete Dunham in Green Street (2005), Jackson "Jax" Teller in the FX drama series Sons of Anarchy (2008–2014) for which he was twice nominated for the Critics' Choice Television Award for Best Actor in a Drama Series,  Raleigh Becket in Pacific Rim (2013), Percy Fawcett in The Lost City of Z (2017), and the title role in Guy Ritchie's King Arthur: Legend of the Sword (2017).
