Roman Vatslavovich Malinovsky (Russian: Рома́н Ва́цлавович Малино́вский, 1876–1918) was a prominent Bolshevik politician before the revolution, while at the same time working as the best paid agent for the Okhrana. They codenamed him  'Portnoi' (the tailor).
He was a brilliant orator, tall, red-haired, yellow-eyed and pockmarked.
