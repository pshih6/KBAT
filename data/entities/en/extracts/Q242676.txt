Liu Yifei (Chinese: 刘亦菲; pinyin: Liú Yìfēi, born An Feng August 25, 1987), also known as Crystal Liu, is a Chinese-American actress, singer, and model. She is widely known as Fairy Sister in the entertainment industry. She was named as one of the New Four Dan actresses of China in 2009. She played the lead role as Mulan in The Walt Disney Company's live-action film Mulan.
