Robert Ellsworth (born May 3, 1959), best known as Bobby "Blitz" Ellsworth, is the lead vocalist of New Jersey thrash metal band Overkill. He has been the lead vocalist of Overkill since its inception in 1980, and he and bassist D.D. Verni are the only constant members. Although a bassist, he focuses on vocals in the band.
He also did the vocals for the band The Cursed featuring Dan Lorenzo (Hades and Non Fiction) in 2007, and is a member of the supergroup/cover band BPMD.
