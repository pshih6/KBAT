Daniel Andrew "Dan" Wells (born March 4, 1977) is an American horror and science fiction author.  He is noted for his John Cleaver series, Mirador series, and New York Times bestselling Partials Sequence. His first published novel I Am Not a Serial Killer became a major motion picture in 2016. He currently co-hosts Writing Excuses, a podcast that answers writing-related questions. He is a Utah native and a graduate of Brigham Young University.
