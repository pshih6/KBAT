Anatoliy Eduardovich Serdyukov (Russian: Анатолий Эдуардович Сердюко́в; born 8 January 1962) is a Russian politician and businessman who was the Minister of Defense of Russia from 15 February 2007 to 6 November 2012. During his tenure as defense minister, he launched several major reforms of the Russian military. Since October 2015 he works as an Industrial Director for Rostec State corporation.
