Ekrem Jevrić (Cyrillic: Екрем Јеврић, pronounced [ěkrem jěːʋritɕ]; born 25 October 1961 – 4 March 2016) was a Montenegrin American singer and television personality based in Yonkers, New York. Recognized as an internet phenomenon by the BBC and The Independent, he rose to fame with his viral music video "Kuća poso" (House - Work) in 2010. The camp song, which has garnered over two million views during the first month and over 15 million overall, earned him instant popularity across former Yugoslavia.
