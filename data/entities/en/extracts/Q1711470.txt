Srđan "Žika" Todorović (Serbian Cyrillic: Срђан "Жика" Тодоровић; born 28 March 1965) is a Serbian actor and musician. Todorović has served as a member of numerous bands, including Ekatarina Velika, Disciplina Kičme, Radnička Kontrola and Bezobrazno Zeleno.
Žika is the son of Serbian actor Bora Todorović and his first wife Snežana Matić.
He is married to Ana, with whom he had a son Dejan who died in late 2017 at three years of age.
