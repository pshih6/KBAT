Anne-Marie Johnson (born July 18, 1960) is an American actress and impressionist. She is known for her roles as Nadine Hudson Thomas in the sitcom What's Happening Now!! (1985-1988), and Althea Tibbs in the NBC police procedural In the Heat of the Night (1988-1993). She also was a cast member in the Fox sketch comedy In Living Color (1993-94) during its final season, and had a recurring and regular roles in Melrose Place, JAG, Girlfriends and The InBetween.
