Jona, Baron von Ustinov (Russian: Иона Платонович Устинов, romanized: Iona  Platonovich Ustinov; 2 December 1892 – 1 December 1962), also known as Klop Ustinov (Клоп Устинов), was a German journalist and diplomat who worked for MI5 during the time of the Nazi regime. His father was the Russian-born emigre Baron Plato von Ustinov  (1840–1918). His son was the actor Sir Peter Ustinov.
