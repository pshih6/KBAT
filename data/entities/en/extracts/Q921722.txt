Giulietto Chiesa (Italian pronunciation: [dʒuˈljetto ˈkjɛːza]; born September 1940) is an Italian journalist, author, lecturer and politician.He was Vice-President of the European Parliament's Committee on International Trade and member of two Extraordinary Committees inside the European Parliament: the Extraordinary Renditions Committee and the Climate Change Committee. He was the founder of the cultural association Megachip. Democracy in Communications. He is the Chief Editor of the web TV Pandora TV.
