Mukesh Dhirubhai Ambani (born 19 April 1957) is an Indian billionaire business magnate, and the chairman, managing director, and largest shareholder of Reliance Industries Ltd. (RIL), a Fortune Global 500 company and India's most valuable company by market value. As of March 2020, Mukesh Ambani is the second richest Asian after Chineese business magnate Jack Ma.
