The Duchess and the Dirtwater Fox is a 1976 American western romantic comedy film starring Goldie Hawn and George Segal, produced, directed and co-written by Melvin Frank. It is about an 1880s dance hall girl (Hawn) from San Francisco who steals a satchel of ill-gotten money as part of her plan to change her identity into an Englsh governess and get a job with a wealthy family in Utah. She then has to elude the former owners of the money, the Bloodhound Gang, on a cross-country chase.
