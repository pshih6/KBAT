Bandar bin Abdulaziz Al Saud (Arabic: بندر بن عبد العزيز آل سعود‎, 1923 – 28 July 2019) was a Saudi royal and the tenth son of Ibn Saud, the first monarch of Saudi Arabia. At the time of his death, he was the eldest surviving member of the ruling branch of the House of Saud (agnatic descendants of Ibn Saud).
