Mohammed VI  (Arabic: محمد السادس‎; Berber languages: ⴰⴳⴳⵍⵉⴷ ⵎⵓⵃⴰⵎⵎⴷ ⵙⴷⵉⵙ; born 21 August 1963) is the King of Morocco. He is a member of the Alaouite dynasty and ascended to the throne on 23 July 1999 upon the death of his father, King Hassan II.In 2019, his net worth was estimated around US$2.1 billion. and he is the Richest King in Africa in 2014 according to the American Business Magazine Forbes.
