Michel Émile Kfoury (Arabic: ميشال اميل كفوري‎; born September 14, 1974), known by his stage name Wael Kfoury (وائل كفوري), is am Arabic (Lebanese) singer, musician, and songwriter. He is considered one of the most popular figures in Arabic music and is often called The King of Romance or simply The King. In a career spanning over 25 years, he has become a Lebanese icon through his romantic songs.
