Val-de-Travers is a municipality in the Val-de-Travers District in the canton of Neuchâtel in Switzerland.  It was created on 1 January 2009, when the former municipalities of Boveresse, Buttes, Couvet, Fleurier, Les Bayards, Môtiers, Noiraigue, Saint-Sulpice and Travers merged to form Val-de-Travers.The region is known for its production of absinthe.
