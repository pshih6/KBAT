Amy Andrea Childs (born 7 June 1990) is an English television personality, businesswoman and model. She appeared in the first two series of the ITV2 reality series The Only Way Is Essex, and then starred in her own reality series It's All About Amy from 2011 to 2012. Childs came in fourth place in the Channel 5 series Celebrity Big Brother 8 in 2011. In 2014, she participated in the Channel 4 series The Jump.
