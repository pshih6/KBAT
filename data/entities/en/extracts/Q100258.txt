Michael Chevalier (born May 14, 1933, died 2005/2006) was a German voice actor born in Berlin. He provided the German dub voices for Charles Bronson (Once Upon a Time in the West), Richard Harris (The Wild Geese), Omar Sharif (Doctor Zhivago), Oliver Reed (Gladiator), Steve McQueen (The Cincinnati Kid), Dan Blocker (Bonanza) and William Conrad (Jake and the Fatman). He also provided dubs for Marlon Brando, Paul Newman and Sean Connery.
He collaborated with the German trance/techno-band E Nomine on a number of their albums.
