William George Zane Jr. (born February 24, 1966) is an American actor. He played antagonist Caledon Hockley in the epic romantic disaster film Titanic (1997). His other films include Back to the Future (1985) and its sequel Back to the Future Part II (1989), Dead Calm (1989), the television series Twin Peaks (1991), Tombstone (1993),  Demon Knight (1995), The Phantom (1996) and the video game Kingdom Hearts (2002).
