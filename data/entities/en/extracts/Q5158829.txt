Concord is an independent music company based in Beverly Hills, California, with worldwide (including the U.S.) distribution through Universal Music Group. The company is specialized in recordings (Concord Records, Fantasy Records, Rounder Records, Telarc Records, Loma Vista Recordings) and music publishing (Boosey & Hawkes, Imagem, Zomba, Harrisongs).
Concord's catalog consists of over 16,000 albums and 390,000 compositions. Concord owns the rights to 273 Grammy-winning albums, and over 400 certifications across 215 titles by the RIAA.
