William Edward Fichtner Jr. (born November 27, 1956) is an American actor. Fichtner has appeared in a number of films, TV series and video games. He is known for his roles as Sheriff Tom Underlay on Invasion and Alexander Mahone on Prison Break. His film appearances include Heat, Contact, Armageddon, The Perfect Storm, Go, Crash, Blades of Glory, Black Hawk Down, The Longest Yard, Mr. & Mrs. Smith, The Dark Knight, Date Night, The Lone Ranger, Elysium, Independence Day: Resurgence, and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.
