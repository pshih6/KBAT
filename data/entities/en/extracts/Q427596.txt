A Bullet for the General (Original title: El Chucho Quién Sabe?) is a 1966 Italian Spaghetti Western film directed by Damiano Damiani  starring Gian Maria Volonté, Lou Castel, Klaus Kinski and Martine Beswick. The film tells the story of El Chuncho, a bandit, and Bill Tate (or El Niňo), who is a counter-revolutionary in Mexico. Chuncho soon learns that social revolution is more important than mere money.
