Isabel Gemio Cardoso (born January 5, 1961 in Alburquerque, Badajoz) is a Spanish journalist and radio and television presenter. She was married to a Cuban sculptor (Julio Nilo Manrique Roldán) and has a biological son and an adopted son.She started in Radio Extremadura, and later in Radio Barcelona as Isabel Garbí. Her debut in television was in 1983 with the TVE quiz show Los Sabios.
