Mohammad Taqi Danesh Pajouh (Persian: محمد تقى دانش پژوه‎; born 1911 in Amol and died 1996), was an Iranian scholar and author on Persian and Islamic studies. He served for decades as deputy librarian at Tehran University before joining the Department of History of the Faculty of Theology at that school. In addition to editing and publishing works of others, he authored a number of articles of his own.
