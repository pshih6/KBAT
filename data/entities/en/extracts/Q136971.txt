Mykola Kniazhytskyi (born June 2, 1968 in Lviv) is a Ukrainian journalist, People's Deputy of Ukraine of the VII and VIII and current IV convocations, Head of the Committee on Culture and Spirituality, Co-Head of group for interparliamentary relations with the Republic of Poland, Head of EU-Ukraine Parliamentary Association Committee.
In the past, Mykola Kniazhytskyi held key positions in media companies and Tonis, STB, and UT-1 TV channels, was head of Hazeta 24. Mykola Kniazhytskyi was a member of the National Council of Television and Radio Broadcasting of Ukraine. Kniazhytskyi is a member of the Austrian International Press Institute Austrian International Press Institute. Married, has two sons and a daughter   Mykola Kniazhytskyi is producer of a number of documentaries
