Utoni Daniel Nujoma (born 8 September 1952) is a Namibian politician who has served in various government ministerial positions in the government since 2010. 
Nujoma is also a member of the Central Committee and Politburo of SWAPO. He is the first born son of Namibia's founding President Sam Nujoma, who was in office as president from 1990 to 2005, and Kovambo Nujoma, the former First Lady of Namibia.
