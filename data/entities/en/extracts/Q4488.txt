Clarence John Brown III (born January 5, 1959) is an American actor and voice actor. He is best known for his roles as "Viking" Lofgren in Bad Boys (1983), the Kurgan in Highlander (1986), Byron Hadley in The Shawshank Redemption (1994), Mr. Krabs in SpongeBob SquarePants (1999–present), Brother Justin Crowe on the HBO series Carnivàle (2003–2005) and Kelvin Inman on the ABC series Lost (2004–2010).
