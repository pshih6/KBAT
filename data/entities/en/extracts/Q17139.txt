Akita (秋田市, Akita-shi, Japanese: [aꜜki̥ta]) is the capital city of Akita Prefecture, Japan, and has been designated a core city since 1 April 1997.
As of 1 January 2020, the city has an estimated population of 305,625 in 136628 households, and a population density of 340 persons per km². The total area of the city is 906.07 square kilometres (349.84 sq mi).
