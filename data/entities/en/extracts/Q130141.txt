Lajos Nagy (5 February 1883 - 28 October 1954) was a Hungarian writer. His work covered a number of genres including travel literature. "He came up with his brief, humoristic stories about animals in the beginning of the 1920s and in 1922 a collection of these short humoresques was published under the title Nonsensical Natural History (Képtelen természetrajz)." He joined the Hungarian Communist Party in 1945.
