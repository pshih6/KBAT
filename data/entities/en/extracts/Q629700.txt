Plastic Little (プラスチックリトル, Purasuchikku Ritoru) is an anime created by Satoshi Urushihara.  It was published in Japan as a one-shot OVA. The anime original video animation was first released in North America on VHS by ADV Films, on July 20, 1994. ADV announced that the anime would be a second release on DVD on February 5, 2002. Like much of Urushihara's work, Plastic Little provides significant amounts of fan service, and along with Burn-Up Excess, is one of two ADV titles to include the trademarked "Jiggle Counter".
