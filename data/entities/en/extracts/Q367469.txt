Kadeem Hardison (born July 24, 1965) is an American actor and director. He is best known for his role Dwayne Wayne on A Different World, a spin-off of the long-running NBC sitcom The Cosby Show. He also starred in the Disney Channel series K.C. Undercover as Craig Cooper, the title character's father. Kadeem played Norman in OWN's Love Is before it was abruptly cancelled after renewed for Season 2. As of 2019, he is starring in Showtime drama, Black Monday.
