The Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University (also known as Kellogg) is the business school of Northwestern University, located in the Chicago Metropolitan Area, Illinois. Founded in 1908, Kellogg pioneered the use of group projects and evaluations and popularized the importance of "team leadership" within the business world. Kellogg's 2-Yr MBA Program is currently ranked #3 in the U.S. according to U.S. News & World Report  and Forbes Magazine  and #4 globally according to The Economist.Its main campus is located in Evanston, Illinois, with additional campuses in downtown Chicago, Illinois and Miami, Florida. Kellogg offers MBA, MSM, and PhD graduate programs, along with dual-MBA/JD, dual-MBA/MDI, and MMM programs.
