Matthew Richard Lucas (born 5 March 1974) is an English actor, comedian, and writer.  He is best known for his partnership with David Walliams on the BBC sketch comedy series Rock Profile (1999–2000, 2009), Little Britain (2003–2007), and Come Fly With Me (2010). Lucas played the role of Nardole in the BBC series Doctor Who (2015-2017). He has also appeared in films such as The Infidel (2010), Alice in Wonderland (2010) and its 2016 sequel, Bridesmaids (2011), and Small Apartments (2012).
