Joshua Strickland (born October 23, 1983) is an American singer and actor from Charleston, South Carolina. He graduated from high school at the Charleston County School of the Arts,where he studied vocal with Robin A.Rogers. He attended the College of Charleston, where he studied voice with Deanna McBroom. During 5th grade, Josh was "discovered" by teacher and Producer/Founder/CEO of The Charleston Youth Company, Chuck Long.  He performed with this company for 8 years receiving drama, voice, and dance instruction. This company has been serving the talented students since 1978.
