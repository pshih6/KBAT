Bernard Charlès (born 1957) is a French business executive. He is the chief executive officer and the vice-chairman of the board of directors of Dassault Systèmes, "the 3DEXPERIENCE Company", world leader in 3D design software, 3D digital mock-up and Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) solutions. Charlès is the 13th best-performing CEO in the world according to the Harvard Business Review 2017 ranking. In 2018 Dassault Systèmes was named the most sustainable corporation in the world in the Global 100 Most Sustainable Corporations in the World index by Corporate Knights..  In 2018, he was named one of the "Best CEOs In The World" by the CEOWORLD magazine.
