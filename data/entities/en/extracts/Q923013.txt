Mirosław Orzechowski (Polish pronunciation: [mʲiˈrɔswav ɔʐɛˈxɔfskʲi]; born 28 September 1957 in Łódź) is a Polish politician. He was elected to Sejm on 25 September 2005 getting 3474 votes in 9 Łódź district, as a candidate from the League of Polish Families list. In Jarosław Kaczyński's government he was a deputy minister of national education.
