Konstanz (US: , German: [ˈkɔnstants] (listen), locally: [ˈkɔnʃtants] (listen); English: Constance) is a university city with approximately 83,000 inhabitants located at the western end of Lake Constance in the south of Germany, bordering Switzerland. The city houses the University of Konstanz and was for more than 1200 years residence of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Konstanz.
