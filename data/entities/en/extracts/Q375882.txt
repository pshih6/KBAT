Ahrue Ilustre (born September 17, 1968), commonly known as Ahrue Luster is an American metal guitarist. He was the lead guitarist for the heavy metal band Ill Niño. He is also a former guitarist for Machine Head, as well as Manmade God and a Bay Area thrash metal act called The Horde of Torment who were originally called "Pestilence" but had to change their name because another Pestilence had signed to Roadrunner Records.In 2015, Luster joined the horror Metal band Terror Universal founded by Ill Niño drummer Dave Chavarri. He produced Motograter's second studio album, which has been released in August 11, 2017.

In September 2019 Nonpoint announces he will be their touring lead guitarist for their upcoming tour.
