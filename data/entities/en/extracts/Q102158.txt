Narva (Estonian pronunciation: [ˈnɑrʋɑ]; Russian: Нарва, Russian pronunciation: [ˈnarvə]) is a municipality and city in Estonia.  It is located in Ida-Viru County, at the eastern extreme point of Estonia, at the Russian border, on the Narva River which drains Lake Peipus. The city of Narva is the country's third largest.
