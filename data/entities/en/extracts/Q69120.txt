He was the President of the Senate and Mayor of the Free Hanseatic City of Bremen from 1967 to 1985, the President of the Bundesrat in 1970/71 and 1981/82, and afterwards served as a member of the Bundestag, the German federal parliament, from 1987 to 1998. 
Between 1994 and 1996, Koschnick was the EU administrator of Mostar. He died on 21 April 2016.
