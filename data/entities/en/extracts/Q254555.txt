Stop! Or My Mom Will Shoot is a 1992 American buddy cop action comedy film directed by Roger Spottiswoode and starring Sylvester Stallone and Estelle Getty. The film was released in the United States on February 21, 1992. The film received mostly negative reviews, and was a box office bomb.
In 2006, in response to a question about the films Stallone wished he had not done, this film was the first one he mentioned.
