César Tiempo, born  Israel Zeitlin (March 3, 1906 in Dnipropetrovsk (Yekaterinoslav), Ukraine,
-October 24, 1980)  was a Russian-born screenwriter of Argentine cinema. He wrote the script for award-winning films such as Safo, historia de una pasión  (1943). In 1961 he appeared as an actor in Esta tierra es mía.
