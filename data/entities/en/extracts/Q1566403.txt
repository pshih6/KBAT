Božidar "Boki" Milošević (Serbian Cyrillic: Божидар "Боки" Милошевић; 31 December 1931 – 15 April 2018) was a Serbian clarinetist.
Milošević was born in Prokuplje, Kingdom of Yugoslavia, now Serbia.He completed undergraduate and graduate studies of classical music at the Academy of Music in Belgrade, where he studied with Bruno Brun. He mostly played folk music. He was a member of the Association of Musical Artists of Serbia and was the second clarinetist of the Belgrade Philharmonic Orchestra.
