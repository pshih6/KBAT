Adam Nyerere Bahner (born July 6, 1982), better known by the pseudonym Tay Zonday, is an American singer, musician, announcer, voice artist, actor, comedian, and YouTube personality.He is known for his bass singing voice. He garnered mainstream exposure when his song "Chocolate Rain" became a viral video in July 2007. As of March 2020, "Chocolate Rain" has over 125 million views.
