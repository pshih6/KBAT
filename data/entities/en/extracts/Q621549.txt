Inglewood is a city in southwestern Los Angeles County, California, in the Los Angeles metropolitan area. As of the 2010 U.S. Census, the city had a population of 109,673. It was incorporated on February 14, 1908. The city is in the South Bay region of Los Angeles County. The city will be the home to SoFi Stadium which is currently under construction and when completed in July 2020, it will be the new home of both the National Football League's Los Angeles Rams and Los Angeles Chargers.
