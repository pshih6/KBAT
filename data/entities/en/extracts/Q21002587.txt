Beowulf is a British epic fantasy drama television series broadcast by ITV. It was created by James Dormer, Tim Haines and Katie Newman. Dormer wrote the series based on the poem Beowulf and executive-produced it along with Haines and Newman, while Stephen Smallwood produced the series. The series began airing in the United Kingdom on 3 January 2016 and in the United States from 23 January 2016. The series is based on the Anglo-Saxon poem Beowulf, containing new characters and storylines.
Shortly after the series began, ITV announced that it would not be picked up for another season; the show ended after 13 episodes.
