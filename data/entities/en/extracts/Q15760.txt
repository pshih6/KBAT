Novorossiysk (Russian: Новоросси́йск, IPA: [nəvərɐˈsʲijsk], Adyghe: ЦӀэмэз) is a city in Krasnodar Krai, Russia. It is the country's main port on the Black Sea and the leading Russian port for exporting grain. It is one of the few cities honored with the title of the Hero City. Population: 241,952 (2010 Census); 232,079 (2002 Census); 185,938 (1989 Census).
