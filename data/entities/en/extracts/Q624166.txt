Lee Si-yeon (born Lee Dae-hak on July 24, 1980) is a South Korean transgender actress and model. In 1999, Lee participated in the Anti-Miss Korea competition. In November 2007, Lee changed gender through surgery and became a woman and changed name to Si-yeon. Lee is the niece of Seo Jae-hyuk, a singer-songwriter and guitarist, member of Buhwal.
