Ahmet Muhtar Kent (born December 1, 1952) is an American business executive. He was the chairman and chief executive officer (CEO) of The Coca-Cola Company. He became CEO  in 2008, and chairman in 2009.In December 2016, Coca-Cola announced that Kent would step down as CEO in May 2017, to be replaced by president and COO James Quincey. Kent continued as the company's chairman. He stepped down as chairman in April 2019.
