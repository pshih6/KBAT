María Concepción Alonso Bustillo (born June 29, 1957), better known as María Conchita Alonso, is an actress, singer, songwriter, former beauty queen, and Venezuelan philanthropist.
Throughout her career she has participated in multiple productions both in film and on television, having been nominated for the Independent Spirit Award as Best Leading Actress in 1996 for her role in Caught . As a singer  has received several gold and platinum records, being nominated three times for the Grammy Awards.
María Conchita, was the first Latin American actress not born in the United States to star in a musical play on Broadway, Kiss of the Spider Woman at the Broadhurst Theater in 1995.
