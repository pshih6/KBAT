Yuki Inoue (井上 由起, Inoue Yuki, born May 25, 1970), better known by the stage name Satsuki Yukino (ゆきの さつき, Yukino Satsuki) (previously 雪乃 五月, 雪野 五月), is a Japanese actress, voice actress and narrator. She was a member of Ken Production until 2016.
She played Kagome Higurashi in Inuyasha, Mutsumi Otohime in Love Hina, Yoruichi Shihouin in Bleach, Kaname Chidori in Full Metal Panic!, Mion and Shion Sonozaki in Higurashi When They Cry, and Milly Thompson in Trigun. She appeared as a guest in Animethon 21.
