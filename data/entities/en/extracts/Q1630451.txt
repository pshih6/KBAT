Hospital Records is an independent record label based in South London. Primarily releasing drum and bass, the label was started in 1996 by Tony Colman (London Elektricity) and Chris Goss, and has grown in recent years to become one of the most well known labels within UK dance music. The label has been home to artists such as Danny Byrd, Camo & Krooked, Logistics, Nu:Tone, Metrik, Fred V & Grafix, High Contrast and S.P.Y but also releases tracks from across the DnB genre. In Forest Hill, South London, Hospital also runs the more experimental label Med School Music (until the end of 2019) , the publishing company Songs in the Key of Knife, and their worldwide events brand Hospitality.
From 2009 to 2011 and 2014 to 2019 Hospital Records has won the Best Label title at the Drum and Bass Arena Awards.
