Ghazala Javed (Pashto: غزاله جاويد‎) (January 1, 1988 – June 18, 2012) was a Pakistani Pashtun playback singer from Swat Valley,  Pakistan. She began singing since 2004 and was "popular with young, progressive ethnic Pashtuns in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. Her music was famous not only in Pakistan but also in neighbouring Afghanistan and among Pashtuns around the world.
