Jörg Dräger (born 1 January 1968 in Darmstadt) is a German physicist, politician and manager. From 2001 to 2008 he served as a senator in the Hamburg state government. Since 2008 he has been a member of the Bertelsmann Stiftung Executive Board where he is responsible for the areas of education and integration. He is considered a leading education expert and is the author of numerous books on education policy, including the impacts of digitization.
