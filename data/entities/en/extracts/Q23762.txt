André Honnorat (December 10, 1868 – July 24, 1950) was a French politician. He served as a member of the Chamber of Deputies from 1910 to 1921, and as a member of the French Senate from 1921 to 1945, representing Basses-Alpes.Together with  Émile Deutsch de la Meurthe, he created the Cité Internationale Universitaire de Paris (CIUP), and there is also a residence there which is named after him.
