Franklin Wendell Welker (born March 12, 1946) is an American voice actor, actor, former stand-up comedian and former impressionist, with an entertainment career spanning nearly six decades. He is known for voicing Fred Jones in the Scooby-Doo franchise since its inception in 1969, Scooby-Doo himself since 2002, Megatron and Soundwave in the Transformers franchise, Curious George in the Curious George franchise, Garfield on The Garfield Show, and Nibbler on Futurama, as well as numerous animal vocal effects in many works. In 2016, Welker was honored with an Emmy Award for his lifetime achievement.
