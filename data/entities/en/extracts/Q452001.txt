George Megas Komnenos (Greek: Γεώργιος Μέγας Κομνηνός, Geōrgios Megas Komnēnos), (c. 1255 – after 1284), Emperor of Trebizond from 1266 to 1280. He was the elder son of Emperor Manuel I and his third wife, Irene Syrikaina, a Trapezuntine noblewoman. He succeeded his half-brother Andronikos in 1266 and ruled for 14 years.
