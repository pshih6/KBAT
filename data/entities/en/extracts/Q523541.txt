Hans Edvard Nørregård-Nielsen (born 2 January 1945) is a Danish art historian.
Nørregård-Nielsen was born in Sønder Nissum. He is an alumnus of Ribe Katedralskole and has written several memoirs. In 2001, he was awarded the De Gyldne Laurbær prize for Riber Ret. His 1995 book, Golden Age of Danish Art reviews early nineteenth-century art. He was Chairman of the New Carlsberg Foundation Board until the end of 2013. He is also an adjunct professor of art history at the Aarhus University. He was awarded the Ingenio et Arti gold medal in 2013.
