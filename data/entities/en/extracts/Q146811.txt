Zanesville is a city in and the county seat of Muskingum County, Ohio, United States. It is located 52 miles (84 km) east of Columbus. The population was 25,487 as of the 2010 census. Zanesville anchors the Zanesville Micropolitan Statistical Area (population 86,183) and is part of the Columbus-Marion-Zanesville Combined Statistical Area (population 2,508,498)
