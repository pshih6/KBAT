Thom Barry is an actor who was born in Cleveland, Ohio.  In the 1980s, he was a disc jockey for WUBE-FM in Cincinnati.  He appeared in television advertisements for The Home Depot, Sears, and United Parcel Service, and did voice acting for the TV series The Incredible Hulk and The Wild Thornberrys.  In 2014, The Hollywood Reporter described the actor as best known for playing Detective Will Jeffries on the TV series Cold Case.
