Roton is an independent Romanian record label established in 1994.The company has around 100 employees, having offices in Bucharest, Iași, Cluj and Timișoara. The turnover in 2007 was €4 million. In 2011, Roton Records entered into a licensing deal with Warner Music Group for some of its most popular artists and their catalogue. In October 2015, Warner Music and Roton extended their distribution deal to include Latin America.Roton represents several well-known Romanian artists, such as Iris, Holograf, Proconsul, Akcent, Inna, Mihai Trăistariu and Alexandra Stan.
