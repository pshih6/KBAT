He became well known in 1979 when he was revealed to be the illegitimate son of Prince Henry, the husband of Queen Wilhelmina. This made him a paternal half-brother of Queen Juliana and the half-uncle of Queen Beatrix. 
He became a member of the Centre Party in 1984 and served as chairman until 1986. 
On 18 December 1986 he shot his wife due to her terminal illness. He served four and a half years in prison for murder.
