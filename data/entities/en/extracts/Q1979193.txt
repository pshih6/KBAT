Aleksandr Nikolayevich Tkachov (Russian: Алекса́ндр Никола́евич Ткачёв) (born 23 December 1960) is a Russian politician who has served as Minister of Agriculture of Russia in Dmitry Medvedev's Cabinet from April 2015 to May 2018. Previously he was Governor of Krasnodar Krai in the southern European part of Russia from 2001 to 2015.
