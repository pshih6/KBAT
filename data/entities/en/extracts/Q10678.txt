Yann-Fañch Kemener (April 7, 1957 – March 16, 2019) was a traditional singer from Brittany, born in Sainte-Tréphine (Côtes-d'Armor), France.
He took part in reviving Kan ha diskan (French: chant et contre-chant) in the 1970s and 1980s, especially with  Erik Marchand.  He  collected songs from the oral tradition in the Breton language.He sang in numerous Festoù Noz.
Kemener died in Tréméven on March 16, 2019 at age 61.
