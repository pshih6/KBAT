Raquel Castro (born (1994-11-17)November 17, 1994) is an American actress, singer and songwriter. She is known for starring in the 2004 film Jersey Girl, as Gertie Trinké, the daughter of Ollie Trinké (Ben Affleck) and Gertrude Steiney (Jennifer Lopez), for which Castro won the Young Artist Award for the Best Performance in a Feature Film - Young Actress Age Ten or Younger. She was a contestant in the American version of The Voice.
