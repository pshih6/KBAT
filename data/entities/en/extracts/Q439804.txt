Jill Flint (born November 25, 1977) is an American television and film actress, best known for her role of Jill Casey in the USA Network TV series Royal Pains, and as the popular character Lana Delaney on CBS' award-winning series The Good Wife. She also plays one of the lead characters, Dr. Jordan Alexander, in the NBC medical drama The Night Shift, and a recurring role on Bull, a jury consulting firm as Diana Lindsay.
