Cody R. Simpson (born 11 January 1997) is an Australian singer, songwriter, dancer, actor, and model.Since his debut, he has released three solo studio albums: Paradise (2012), Surfers Paradise (2013), andFree (2015).
He had portrayed the lead role of Dmitry in the Broadway musical Anastasia from November 2018 through April 2019. The same year he won the first season of The Masked Singer (Australia).
