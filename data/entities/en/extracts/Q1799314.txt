Andrew Robert Nielsen (born October 6, 1982), known professionally as MC Lars, is an American rapper, producer, cartoonist and podcaster.  He is one of the self-proclaimed originators of "lit-hop" and was one of the first rappers to sample and reference post-punk, pop punk, and emo bands.He is the founder and CEO of the independent record label Horris Records.  He is one of several people who claim to have coined the term iGeneration, which he is credited with doing in 2003.
