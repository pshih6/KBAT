Michael Sauer (born 27 August 1941 in Recklinghausen) is a German former triple jumper who competed in the 1968 Summer Olympics.Sauer became West German champion every year from 1963 to 1971 except for 1966. Indoors he became West German champion every year from 1963 to 1972, won a national bronze medal every year from 1973 to 1976 and a last gold medal in 1979.
