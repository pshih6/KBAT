Julianna Guill (born July 7, 1987) is an American actress. She is known for her roles as Bree in the 2009 film Friday the 13th, as Scarlet Hauksson in the web series My Alibi, as Becca Riley in Bravo's Girlfriends' Guide to Divorce, and as Jesse Nevin on FOX's The Resident, as well as Christie on the TBS series Glory Daze.
