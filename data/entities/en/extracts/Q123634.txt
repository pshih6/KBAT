Marylebone ( or , , , or ) is an area in the West End of London, England, which has been part of the City of Westminster since 1965, when the latter was enlarged. Bounded by Oxford Street to the south, Marylebone Road to the north, Edgware Road to the west and Great Portland Street to the east, the area east of Great Portland Street up to Cleveland Street, known as Fitzrovia since the 1940s, was historically East Marylebone.
