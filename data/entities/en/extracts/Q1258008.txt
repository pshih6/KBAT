Ajs Nigrutin (born Vladan Aksentijević, Serbian Cyrillic: Владан Аксентијевић, on 12 August 1977 in Belgrade, Serbia, SFR Yugoslavia) is a Serbian rapper.
Known for his nasal baritone vocals, comedic lyrical style and eccentric stage presence, Nigrutin rose to continental prominence as the member of the Belgrade-based recording act Bad Copy. Parallel to his work in Bad Copy, he maintains a successful solo career. His two decade long career proclaimed him commercial acclaim and numerous accolades, including three Serbian Oscars of Popularity, two Melko Awards and an Indexi Award.
