Ran Danker (Hebrew: רן דנקר‎; born Khalil Yosef Danker on January 7, 1984) is an Israeli actor, singer and model. He starred in hit Israeli series such as HaShir Shelanu (2004–2007),  The Gordin Cell (2012–2015), and currently appears in Miguel (2018–present). Danker received an Ophir Award nomination for Best Supporting Actor for his role in Eyes Wide Open (2009).  Danker was also nominated for an Ophir Award for Best Actor for his role in Doubtful (2017).
