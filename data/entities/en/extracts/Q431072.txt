Sean Yazbeck is a British businessman.   He is the winner of the fifth season (2006) of Donald Trump's reality show, The Apprentice.   He is the recipient of the 2014 'British Entrepreneur of The Year Award' from the British Government's, Department for International Trade (previously UKTI).  He is the Founder and CEO of an Inc. 500 Fastest Growing Company in America.   He is the benefactor of 'The Sean Yazbeck Centre for Digital Innovation' at Solent University, a learning centre for BSc, MSc and PhD degrees in media, software and business.
