Vladimir Filat (born 6 May 1969), commonly referred to as Vlad Filat (pronounced [vlad fiËˆlat]), is a Moldovan businessman and politician, founder of Liberal Democratic Party of Moldova. He was the Prime Minister of Moldova from 25 September 2009 to 25 April 2013. He also was appointed as the Interim President of Moldova for a brief period of time in 2010.
