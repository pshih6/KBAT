Ruben Karlenovich Vardanyan (Armenian: Ռուբեն Վարդանյան, Russian: Рубен Карленович Варданян; born May 25, 1968) is an Armenian entrepreneur, Venture philanthropist, and investor, co-founder of Aurora Prize for Awakening Humanity, and UWC Dilijan College. Vardanyan initiated and co-founded Moscow School of Management SKOLKOVO, and held its president’s seat in 2006-2010. He is the also founder of the "Philin" initiative (philanthropy infrastructure), the co-founder of the family welfare service "Phoenix Advisors", and the former CEO and major shareholder of "Troika Dialog" investment bank (1992—2012).
