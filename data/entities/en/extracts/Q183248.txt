Jean Teulé (born 26 February 1953 in Saint-Lô, Manche), is a French novelist, cartoonist and screenwriter. He is the partner of the actress Miou-Miou.
Jean’s book Le magasin des suicides (The Suicide Shop), published in 2007, has been turned into a film called The Suicide Shop.  It screened at the Newport Beach Film Festival in Newport Beach, CA, on Sunday, April 28, 2013 and Thursday, May 2, 2013.
