Andrea Kay Pyle (born November 16, 1972), known professionally as Missi Pyle, is an American actress and singer. She has appeared in a number of successful films, including Galaxy Quest (1999), Big Fish (2003), Bringing Down the House (2003), 50 First Dates (2004), DodgeBall: A True Underdog Story (2004), Charlie and the Chocolate Factory (2005), Harold & Kumar Escape from Guantanamo Bay (2008), The Artist (2011), Gone Girl (2014), Captain Fantastic (2016), Traffik (2018), and Ma (2019). 
Pyle has also appeared in various television series including The Wedding Bells (2007), Cleaners (2013-2014), Jennifer Falls (2014), Another Period (2015-2018), Bordertown (2016), Sing It! (2016), Mom (2017), The Soul Man (2016), and Impulse (2018). 
In addition to acting, Pyle has worked as a musician. With actress Shawnee Smith, she served as half of Smith & Pyle, a country rock band. She also appeared in the music video for Foo Fighters' single "Run".
