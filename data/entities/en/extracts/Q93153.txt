Armen Sarkissian (Armenian: Արմեն Վարդանի  Սարգսյան; born 23 June 1953) is an Armenian politician, physicist and computer scientist who has served as the President of Armenia since 2018. He served as Prime Minister of Armenia from 4 November 1996 to 20 March 1997 and previously was the country's (longest-serving) ambassador in London since 1998. Sarkissian was elected on 2 March 2018 and assumed the presidency on 9 April 2018.
