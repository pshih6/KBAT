Julieta Susana Gonzalo (Spanish pronunciation: [ˈʝuli ɣonˈsalo]; born September 9, 1981) is an Argentine actress. She is best known for playing Pamela Rebecca Barnes on the television soap opera Dallas (2012–2014). She has also appeared in films such as Freaky Friday (2003), A Cinderella Story (2004) and Christmas with the Kranks (2004).
