Géza Kiss (22 October 1882 – 23 August 1952) was a Hungarian freestyle swimmer who competed in the 1904 Summer Olympics and 1906 Intercalated Games.
In the 1904 Olympics he won a silver medal in the 1 mile freestyle and a bronze medal in the 880 yard freestyle.
Two years later, at the Intercalated Games, he won a gold medal as a member of Hungarian 4×250 m freestyle relay.
