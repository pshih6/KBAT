David Pender Richards  (born 3 June 1952) is the chairman of Prodrive, chairman of Motorsport UK (The Royal Automobile Club Motor Sports Association Ltd), former chairman of Aston Martin, a former team principal of the BAR and Benetton Formula One motor racing teams and World Rally Champion in 1981. He lives with his wife, Karen, in Warwickshire and has three children.
