Zhao Jingshen (simplified Chinese: 赵景深; traditional Chinese: 趙景深; pinyin: Zhào Jǐngshēn) (April 25, 1902 – January 7, 1985) was a popular Chinese novelist. Born in Lishui, Zhejiang, he was a member of the Seminar in literature. He also contributed to the field of translation and folk opera  and made a great success.  He also funded some outstanding writers.
