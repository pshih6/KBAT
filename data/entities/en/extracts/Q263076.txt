Robin Caspar Walker (born 12 April 1978) is a British Conservative Party politician who has been a Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State serving jointly between the Scotland Office and the Northern Ireland Office in the UK Government since his appointment by Prime Minister Boris Johnson on 26 July 2019.
Since 2010 he has been the Member of Parliament (MP) for Worcester. His father was Lord Walker of Worcester, who was the MP for Worcester from 1961 to 1992.
He identifies as a One-Nation Conservative.
