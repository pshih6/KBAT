Kevin Wayne Yoder (born January 8, 1976) is an American politician who served as a member of the United States House of Representatives for Kansas's 3rd congressional district from 2011 to 2019. A Republican, Yoder was the Kansas State Representative for the 20th district from 2003 to 2011.
In his 2018 reelection bid, he was defeated by Democrat Sharice Davids.
