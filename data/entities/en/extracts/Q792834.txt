Ğayaz İsxaqıy aka Ayaz İshaki (Tatar: Cyrillic Гаяз Исхакый, Latin Ğayaz İsxaqí, Russian: Гаяз Исхаки, Gajaz Ishaki) (10 February 1878 — 22 July 1954) was a leading figure of the Volga Bulgarian national movement, prominent Bulgarian writer, journalist, publisher, close associate of Sadri Maksudy in the creation of the first republic of the Volga Bulgarians - Idel-Ural.
