Christina Ann McNichol (born September 11, 1962) is an American actress, comedian, producer, and singer.She is known for such roles as Angel in the film Little Darlings, Polly in the film Only When I Laugh, and Barbara Weston in the TV sitcom Empty Nest. She won two Emmy Awards for her portrayal of teenage daughter Letitia "Buddy" Lawrence in the TV drama Family.
In 1998, she retired from acting.
