See other locations named Teplice.Teplice (Czech pronunciation: [ˈtɛplɪtsɛ]), Teplice-Šanov until 1948 (Czech pronunciation: [ˈtɛplɪtsɛ ˈʃanof]; German: Teplitz-Schönau), is a statutory city in the Ústí nad Labem Region of the Czech Republic and the capital of the Teplice District. It is Czech Republic's second largest spa town, after Karlovy Vary.
