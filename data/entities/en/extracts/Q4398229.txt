The Russian state (Российское государство, Rossiyskoye gosudarstvo) — is a state proclaimed by the Act of the Ufa State Conference of September 23, 1918 (the Constitution of the Provisional All-Russian Government) “on the formation of the all-Russian supreme power” in the name of “restoring state unity and independence of Russia” affected by the revolutionary events of 1917, the establishment of Soviet power and the signing of the Soviet government of the treaty of Brest-Litovsk.
