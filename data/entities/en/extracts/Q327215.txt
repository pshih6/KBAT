Hal Scardino (born Albert Henry Hugh Scardino; December 25, 1984) is an American actor and producer best known for having played the leading role in the movie The Indian in the Cupboard. He also starred in Searching for Bobby Fischer, Marvin's Room, and The Show (2015).
Scardino will appear in the play Saltonstall's Trial by Michael Cormier in October 2019 in Beverly, MA.
