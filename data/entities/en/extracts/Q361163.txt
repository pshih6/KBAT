Yevgeny Ananyevich Khaldei (Russian: Евгений Ананьевич Халдей)  (23 March [O.S. 10 March] 1917 – 6 October 1997) was a Red Army naval officer and photographer, best known for his World War II photograph of a Soviet soldier Raising a flag over the Reichstag, in Berlin, capital of the vanquished Nazi Germany.
