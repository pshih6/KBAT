Zamaana Deewana (English: Crazy Times) is a 1995 Bollywood comedy film, produced by G. P. Sippy under the Sippy Films banner and directed by Ramesh Sippy. It stars Shahrukh Khan, Raveena Tandon in the pivotal roles along with Jeetendra, Shatrughan Sinha playing their parents, respectively and music composed by Nadeem-Shravan. The film is recorded as a disaster at the box office.
