Latifa bint Abdulaziz is a member of the House of Saud. Her mother was Hassa bint Ahmed Al Sudairi, a member of the Al Sudairi family that is one of the most powerful families in Nejd. Her father was King Abdulaziz. She was one of their eleven children together, a full-sister of the Sudairi Seven, and sister to Kings Fahd and Salman. Latifa patronised universities.
