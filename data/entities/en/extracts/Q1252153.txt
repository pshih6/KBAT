Dragan Marković (Serbian Cyrillic: Драган Марковић; born 2 May 1960), and commonly known as Palma ("palm tree"), is a Serbian businessman, politician and entrepreneur. He currently serves as a member of the National Assembly of Serbia and is a founder and the party leader of United Serbia (Jedinstvena Srbija).
He was the mayor of Jagodina from 2004 until 2012. In February 2001, he was appointed Deputy Minister of Agriculture, Forestry and Water Management in the first post-Milošević government of Serbia.
