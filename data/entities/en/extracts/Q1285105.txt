Milojko "Mile" Kitić (Serbian Cyrillic: Милојко „Миле” Китић; born 1 January 1952) is a Bosnian-born Serbian singer. He rose to prominence as a member of the popular eighties turbo-folk collective Južni Vetar with fellow folk singers Sinan Sakić, Dragana Mirković, Kemal Malovčić and Šemsa Suljaković.
