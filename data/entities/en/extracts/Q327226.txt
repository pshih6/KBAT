Will Sanderson (born May 26, 1980) is a Canadian television and film actor.
He is known for having a long relationship with director Uwe Boll, having appeared in most of his video game-based movies, such as House of the Dead, Alone in the Dark, BloodRayne and In the Name of the King.
In 2007, he played the leading role in Uwe Boll's horror movie Seed, alongside Michael Paré and Ralf Möller.
He is now an attending Emergency Medicine physician, medical educator, and podcaster.
