Suleka "Sue" Mathew is a Canadian actress, known for her portrayal of Dr. Sunita Ramen on the Canadian television series Da Vinci's Inquest.She was born in India and raised in Vancouver, British Columbia, since the age of two.She appeared for two seasons on the ABC sitcom Men in Trees playing the character Sara Jackson. She next played a nurse named Bobbie Jackson on the TNT series HawthoRNe, which began in 2009, and ran for three seasons.  In 2013 she played Dina Tomlin on the U.S. TV series, Red Widow. She is currently playing Arlene Branch in the TNT show Claws.
