Seychelle Suzanne Gabriel (born  March 25, 1991) is an American actress. On television, she portrayed Lourdes Delgado in Falling Skies (2011–2014) and voiced Asami Sato in The Legend of Korra (2012–2014). She has also appeared in the films The Spirit (2008), The Last Airbender (2010), Honey 2 (2011), Sleight (2016), and Blood Fest (2018).
