Teijo Eloranta (born 3 January 1960 in Helsinki, Finland) is a Finnish actor and television writer.
Eloranta began his career in 1991 in television appearing chiefly on TV between then and 2003. However, since 2003 he has increasingly moved into film his most recent in the 2006 film Saippuaprinssi  in which he worked with actors such as Mikko Leppilampi and Pamela Tola.
