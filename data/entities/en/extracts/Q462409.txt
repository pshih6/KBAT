The Way of the Dragon (Chinese: 猛龍過江, originally released in the United States as Return of the Dragon) is a 1972 Hong Kong martial arts action comedy film written, co-produced and directed by Bruce Lee, who also stars in the lead role. This is Lee's only complete directorial film. The film co-stars Nora Miao, Robert Wall, Wei Ping-ou, with Chuck Norris playing his debut screen role. Way of the Dragon was released in Hong Kong on 30 December 1972.
