Sebastià Juan Arbó (Catalan pronunciation: [səβəstiˈa ʒuˈan əɾˈβo]; 1902–1984) was a Spanish novelist and playwright. He wrote in Catalan and Spanish. He was born in Sant Carles de la Ràpita on October 28, 1902, and died in Barcelona on January 2, 1984. His work includes novels, drama, biographies and translations. He was an honorary member of the Association of Writers in the Catalan Language. In 1948 he won the Premio Nadal (the Nadal Prize).
