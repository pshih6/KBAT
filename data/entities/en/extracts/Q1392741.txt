Fahrudin Radončić (pronounced [rǎdoːnt͡ʃit͡ɕ]; born 24 May 1957) is a Bosnian media magnate, entrepreneur, investor, and politician who is the 6th and current Minister of Security. He is the founder of the Dnevni avaz, the most influential and best-selling newspaper in Bosnia and Herzegovina and is the founder and current president of the Union for a Better Future of BiH (SBB BiH).
Radončić already served as Minister of Security once, from 22 November 2012 until 29 April 2014. From 2015 to 2019, he was also the member of the House of Peoples of Bosnia and Herzegovina.
