Cary-Hiroyuki Tagawa (JP: 田川 洋行 Tagawa Hiroyuki, RU: Пантелеймон Тагава Panteleymon Tagawa; born September 27, 1950) is a Japanese-American actor known for his role as Nobusuke Tagomi in The Man in the High Castle, the Amazon original series adaptation of the novel by Philip K. Dick.
His roles have included Chang in The Last Emperor, the voice of Sin Tzu for the video game Batman: Rise of Sin Tzu, the evil soul-stealing sorcerer Shang Tsung in a film adaptation of the video game Mortal Kombat (a role he would reprise 18 years later for Mortal Kombat: Legacy and 24 years later in Mortal Kombat 11) and the evil mastermind Heihachi Mishima in the film adaptation of Tekken.
