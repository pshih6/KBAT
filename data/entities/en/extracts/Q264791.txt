Jonathan Bar Giora (Hebrew: יונתן בר גיורא‎; born 8 July 1962) is an Israeli composer and pianist. 
Since 2000, Bar Giora has composed scores and soundtracks for Israeli films such as Bonjour Monsieur Shlomi, Time of Favor and Aviva, My Love. He also worked as a composer with Israeli actors such as Yossi Banai, Rita and Rickie Gal. Bar Giora lectures at the Sapir Academic College and at Maale Film School.
