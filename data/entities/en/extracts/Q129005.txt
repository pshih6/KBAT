Óscar Raymundo Benavides Larrea (March 15, 1876 – July 2, 1945) was a prominent Peruvian field marshal, diplomat, and politician who served as the 45th (1914 – 1915, by a coup d'etat) and 49th (1933 – 1939) President of Peru.
He is also the credited with the quote: "For my friends everything, for my enemies the law".
