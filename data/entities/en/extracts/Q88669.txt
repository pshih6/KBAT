Hubert Burda (born 9 February 1940) is a German billionaire publisher. He is the owner, publisher and general partner of Hubert Burda Media, a global media company of more than 600 media products, including websites, print magazines and other brands. It operates in 20 countries, predominantly in Germany and the UK. Its brands include Focus, Bunte and Radio Times.Burda is chairman of the conference Digital Life Design (DLD), which takes place annually in January in Munich.
Forbes Magazine estimates his net worth at $4 billion.
