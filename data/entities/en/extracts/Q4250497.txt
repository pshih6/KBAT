Saira Khan (born 15 May 1970) is a British television presenter. In 2005, she was the runner-up of The Apprentice. From 2012 to 2017, Khan co-presented The Martin Lewis Money Show and in 2015, she presented the ITV series Guess This House., currently she runs a skincare  business called Saira Skin, she also is a panelist on Loose Women and competed in Celebrity Big Brother 18. In 2019, she competed in Dancing on Ice.
