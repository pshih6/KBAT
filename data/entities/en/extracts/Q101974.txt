Joseph-Ernst Graf Fugger von Glött , since 1940: Fürst Fugger von Glött (October 26, 1895, Kirchheim in Schwaben – May 13, 1981, Miesbach)  was a German politician and representative of the Christian Social Union of Bavaria. He was a member of the Bundestag of Germany between 1949 and 1953. From 1954 to 1962 he was a member of the Landtag of Bavaria.  He is a member of the famed Fugger family, the preeminent bankers of the renaissance era.
