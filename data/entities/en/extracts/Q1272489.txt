Ralph Michael Ineson (born 15 December 1969) is an English actor. Known for his deep, rumbling voice, his most notable roles include William in The Witch, Dagmer Cleftjaw in Game of Thrones, Amycus Carrow in the last three Harry Potter films, Donald Bamford in the BBC drama series Goodnight Sweetheart, and Chris Finch in the BBC sitcom The Office.
