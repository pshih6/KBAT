He also published 'Historiae Musulmanae Turcorum, de monumentis ipsorum exscriptae, libri XVIII.'
Leunclavius accompanied the nobleman Heinrich von Lichtenstein on a diplomatic mission to Istanbul, staying there from October 1584 to April 1585.He was a friend of Friedrich Sylburg, who published his translation of Zosimus.
