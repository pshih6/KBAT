John Patrick Amedori (born April 20, 1987) is an American actor and musician. He is best known for playing Gabe Mitchell in Dear White People.
Amedori has also appeared in a number of television shows, including Law & Order, Nip/Tuck, Joan of Arcadia and Ghost Whisperer, as well as a part in the films The Butterfly Effect and  Stick It. In 2006, Amedori starred in Fox's drama series Vanished, and in 2008 was cast as Aaron Rose in Gossip Girl as a love interest of Serena van der Woodsen and the stepbrother of Blair Waldorf. Amedori stars as Gabe Mitchell in the Netflix television series Dear White People.
