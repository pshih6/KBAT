The New Azerbaijan Party (Azerbaijani: Yeni Azərbaycan Partiyası, YAP) is the ruling political party in Azerbaijan, founded on November 21, 1992 under leadership of Heydar Aliyev. After his election as President of Azerbaijan on October 3, 1993, and party's victory at 1995 parliamentary elections, YAP became a ruling party. President Ilham Aliyev has been Chairman of YAP since its 3rd Congress held on March 26, 2005.
YAP is a member of the International Conference of Asian Political Parties (ICAPP) and an Observer member of the Centrist Democrat International.
