Rustem Rinatovich Adagamov (Russian: Русте́м Рина́тович Адага́мов) is an influential Russian blogger who goes by the pseudonym of "Drugoi" ("Другой"), meaning "Another". The Daily Telegraph has described Adagamov as "Russia's number one blogger".In 2009 Kremlin's press department invited him to cover a meeting between Dmitry Medvedev and his visiting Indian counterpart Pratibha Patil.
