Susan Diane Wojcicki ( wuu-CHITS-kee; born July 5, 1968) is an American technology executive. She has been the CEO of YouTube since February 2014.Wojcicki was involved in the founding of Google, and became Google's first marketing manager in 1999. She later led the company's online advertising business and was put in charge of Google's original video service. After observing the success of YouTube, Wojcicki proposed the acquisition of YouTube by Google in 2006.
Wojcicki has an estimated net worth of nearly $500 million.
