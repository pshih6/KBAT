Xuzhou, known as Pengcheng in ancient times, is a major city in Jiangsu province, China. The city, with a recorded population of 8,577,225 at the 2010 census (2,623,066 of which lived in the built-up area), is a national complex transport hub. Xuzhou is a central city of Huaihai Economic Zone and Xuzhou Metropolitan Circle.The city is designated as National Famous Historical and Cultural City since 1986 for its relics, especially the terracotta armies, the Mausoleums of the princes and the art of relief of Han dynasty.
Xuzhou is an important node city of the country ’s “The Belt and Road”, a central city in the Huaihai Economic Zone, an important central city in the northern wing of the Yangtze River Delta, a core city in the Xuzhou metropolitan area, and an international new energy base. Xuzhou has won titles such as the National Civilized City and the United Nations Habitat Award.
Xuzhou, one of the biggest cities in China and Jiangsu Province, is an important gateway city in East China.
