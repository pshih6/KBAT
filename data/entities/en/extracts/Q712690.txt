Talal bin Abdulaziz Al Saud (Arabic: طلال بن عبد العزيز آل سعود‎; 15 August 1931 – 22 December 2018), formerly also called The Red Prince, was a senior member of the Saudi royal family. He was notable for his liberal stance, striving for a national Constitution, the full rule of law and equality before the law. He was also the leader of Free Princes Movement.
