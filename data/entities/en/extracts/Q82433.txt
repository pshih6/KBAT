Fusanosuke Natsume (夏目 房之介, Natsume Fusanosuke, born August 18, 1950) is a Japanese columnist and cartoonist. Born in Tokyo to Jun'ichi Natsume, son of novelist Natsume Sōseki, he attended Aoyama Gakuin University, where he graduated in 1973.  
He was awarded the Tezuka Osamu Cultural Prize (Special Award) in 1999 for the excellence criticism of manga.He has written the book Manga wa ima dou natteorunoka? (マンガは今どうなっておるのか?) (published in 2005), which was illustrated by Fumi Yoshinaga.
