Alessio Saro (born May 30, 1975), (also known with the name of Billy Ballo and Nick Malanno), is an Italian actor and comedian. He works in Shortcut Production performing in comedy sketches with Marcello Macchia, Enrico Venti, Luigi Luciano and Franco Mari. In May 2009 he was arrested for having sex with a girl of thirteen. He was  sentenced to four months in prison. After serving his sentence, he returned to acting in the TV movie Drammi Medicali (Medical Dramas).
