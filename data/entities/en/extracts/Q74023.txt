Lyudmyla Leontiivna Denisova (Ukrainian: Людмила Леонтіївна Денісова; Russian: Людмила Леонтьевна Денисовна) (born July 6, 1960 in Arkhangelsk, Russian SFSR) is a Ukrainian politician and former Minister of Labor and Social Policy who on 15 March 2018 was elected Commissioner for Human Rights in Ukraine.
