Viktor Palokaj (born March 18, 1981), known professionally as Unikkatil (stylized as UniKKatil), is an Albanian rapper, singer, songwriter and record producer who lives in Bronx, New York. He was born in Prishtina, Kosovo and his origin is from Kaçinar, Mirditë. With all messages which he expresses during his songs, he is considered to be the greatest Albanian rapper of all time.
