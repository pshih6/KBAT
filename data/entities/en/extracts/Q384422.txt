Patrizio Prata (born 2 February 1972 in Milan) is an Italian voice actor and dubbing director who contributes to voicing characters in movies, cartoons, anime, video games and more content.Prata is well known for voicing characters in animated content such as Android #17 in Dragon Ball Z, Roronoa Zoro in One Piece, Kabuto Yakushi in Naruto and Naruto: Shippuden, Jaden Yuki in Yu-Gi-Oh! GX and other famous animated titles to date. He also provides the voice of Geronimo Stilton in the Geronimo Stilton animated series.
Prata currently works at Studio P.V., Deneb Film, Merak Film and other dubbing studios in Italy. Since February 2007, he is also the current voice-over host for Disney Channel Italy.
