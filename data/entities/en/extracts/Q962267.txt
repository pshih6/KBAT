Papa to Kiss in the Dark (パパとKISS IN THE DARK) is a Japanese yaoi light novel written by Ken Nanbara and illustrated by Sae Momoki. It has been adapted into an anime OVA series animated by TNK. Several drama CDs also based on this light novel have been released.
In June 2008, the anime was licensed for release in English by Media Blasters under their Kitty Media label.
