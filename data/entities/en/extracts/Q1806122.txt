Larry Thrasher (born 1959) is an American experimental musician, remixer, and producer. [1] Thrasher was a member of Psychic TV, Thee Majesty and Splinter Test. In addition, Thrasher was in collaboration with Kim Cascone on the experimental noise project Thessalonians. He is credited under the aliases DJ Cheb I Sabbah Wa Mektoub, Baba Larry Ji. [2], and Larriji. [3] He is also a long time follower of Meher Baba.
