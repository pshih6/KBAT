James E. Moore Jr. (born 1951) is an American composer, singer and music pedagogue from LaCrosse, Virginia.Moore was professor of music and liturgy at the Athenaeum in Ohio between 1977 and 1982, and music director of the Chor der Alten Burse in Vienna from 1984 to 1987. Since 1984 he has lived in Vienna, where he gives private lessons in singing and conducting.
