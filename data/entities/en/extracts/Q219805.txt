Zombie Holocaust (Italian: Zombi Holocaust) is a 1980 Italian horror film directed by Marino Girolami. The film is about a team of scientists who follow a trail of corpses to a pacific island where they meet a mad doctor (Donald O'Brien) who performs experiments on  both the living and dead in his laboratory. The team begins to become victims of the doctor and his zombie horde while trying to escape from cannibals as well. The film was re-edited and released theatrically in the United States in 1982 as Doctor Butcher M.D.
