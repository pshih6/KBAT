Kherson (Ukrainian: Херсо́н, IPA: [xerˈsɔn] (listen); Russian: Херсо́н, IPA: [xʲɪrˈson]) is a city in southern Ukraine. It functions as the  administrative center of Kherson Oblast (province). Designated as a city of oblast significance. Kherson is an important port on the Black Sea and on the Dnieper River, and the home of a major ship-building industry. As of 2015 it had a population of 294,941 (2015 est.).
Since 2014, Kherson has housed the office of the Ukrainian President's representative in the Crimea, which is headed by Nataliya Popovych.
