Dragons Forever (Chinese: 飛龍猛將) is a 1988 Hong Kong action comedy film directed by Sammo Hung and Corey Yuen. It stars Sammo Hung, Jackie Chan and Yuen Biao, with Deannie Yip, Pauline Yeung, and Crystal Kwok. It is the last film that Hung, Chan and Biao all have appeared in together, as Chan focused on his solo film career.
