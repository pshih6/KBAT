Issei Nishikawa (西川 一誠, Nishikawa Issei, born 2 January 1945) is a Japanese politician and the incumbent governor of Fukui Prefecture in Japan. He was elected first in 2003. A native of Asahi, Fukui and graduate of Kyoto University with the B.L. degree in 1968, he joined the Ministry of Home Affairs in 1968.
