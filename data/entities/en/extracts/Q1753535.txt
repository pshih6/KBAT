The Royal College of Art (RCA) is a public research university in London,  United Kingdom, with campuses in South Kensington, Battersea and White City. The only entirely postgraduate art and design university in the world,:118 it offers postgraduate degrees in art and design to students from over 60 countries.  As of 2020, the RCA has placed first in the QS World University Rankings in the Art and Design subject area for six consecutive years, since the introduction of subject area rankings in 2014.
