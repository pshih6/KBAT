Accident is Harold Pinter's 1967 British dramatic film adaptation of the 1965 novel by Nicholas Mosley.  Directed by Joseph Losey, it was the second of three collaborations between Pinter and Losey, the others being The Servant (1963) and The Go-Between (1970).
