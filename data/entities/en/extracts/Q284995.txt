Laouni Mouhid (Arabic: العوني محيد‎, pronounced [laːwniː muːħiːd]; born December 25, 1981 in Trappes, Île-de-France), commonly known by his stage name La Fouine (French pronunciation: ​[la ˈfwin]; lit. 'The Weasel'), is a French-Moroccan rapper, singer-songwriter and record producer.
He is owner of Banlieue Sale and clothing line "Street Swagg".
