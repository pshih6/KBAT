San Luis Obispo (; SLO () for short) is a city in the U.S. state of California, located approximately 190 miles (310 km) north of Los Angeles and 230 miles (370 km) south of San Francisco in Northern California's Central Coast region. The population was 45,119 at the 2010 census. The population of San Luis Obispo County was 269,637 in 2010.
