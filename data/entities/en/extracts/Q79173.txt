Eugenie Schwarzwald, née Nußbaum,  was born 4 July 1872, in Polupanivka near Zbruch River in Austria-Hungary (now Pidvolochysk Raion, Ukraine) and died on 7 August 1940, in Zurich. She was an Austrian philanthropist, writer and pedagogue developing and supporting education for girls in Austria. She was one of the most lettered women of her time.
