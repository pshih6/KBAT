Alicia Koplowitz y Romero de Juseu, 7th Marchioness of Bellavista, G.d.E (born 12 September 1954) is a Spanish business magnate and noblewoman. When her father died, she and her sister inherited Construcciones y Contratas, S.A. (CYCSA), a company founded by her father. She then sold her part of the company to her sister and created one of the largest family offices in Europe called Omega Capital. She ranks as Spain's richest woman in Forbes 2013 World's Richest People list (#589 in the world).
