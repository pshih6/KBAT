Robert Lindsay (born 13 December 1949) is an English stage and TV actor. He is mainly known for his work in sitcoms, most notable as Ben Harper in My Family for which he played the role for over a decade. His appearances with the Royal Shakespeare Company and in musical theatre, and his roles as the narrator on the TV adaptations of the children's television series Brambly Hedge. His first major role on TV was playing Wolfie Smith in Citizen Smith.  He has won a BAFTA, a Tony Award, and three Olivier Awards for his work.
