David Kenneth Harbour (born April 10, 1975) is an American actor. He began his film career in 2004, and went on to star in supporting roles in films such as Quantum of Solace, The Green Hornet, End of Watch, The Equalizer and Black Mass. Harbour portrayed the title character in the 2019 Hellboy reboot, and gained recognition for his portrayal of Jim Hopper in Stranger Things, which earned him a Critics' Choice Television Award in 2018. He has also received Primetime Emmy Award nominations and Golden Globe Award nominations for the role.
