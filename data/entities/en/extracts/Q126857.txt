Milka Loff Fernandes (born 19 June 1980 in Hamburg) is a German TV presenter and actress of Cape Verdean descent.
Fernandes was, from 1999 until 2004, VJ for the television channel Viva and in 2002 recorded the song Girl for a day with Band Ohne Namen, reaching Number 16 in the German music charts.Fernandes has also played roles in the television crime series Tatort, Rosa Roth and the short film Traum(a).
