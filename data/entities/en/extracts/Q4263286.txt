Sergey Fyodorovich Lisovsky (Russian: Серге́й Фёдорович Лисо́вский; born 25 April 1960, Moscow) is a Russian politician, a member of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation since 2004, a representative of the Kurgan Regional Duma. TV host channel RBC TV. In the past - the entrepreneur. Candidate of Philology (2000). Member of the General Council of the party United Russia. It is one of the pioneers of the Russian social and political advertisements and an acknowledged expert on the contemporary electoral technologies.
