Leif Valdemar Johansson (born 30 September 1951), is a Swedish businessman. He was President and CEO of the Volvo Group from 1997 to 2011 and Chairman of Ericsson from 2011 until 2017 and is currently Chairman of AstraZeneca plc.
In 2012, the Fokus Magazine ranked him as Sweden's 6th most powerful person.
