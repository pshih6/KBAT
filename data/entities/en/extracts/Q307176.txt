Abd el-Aziz el-Zoubi (Arabic: عبد العزيز الزعبي‎, Hebrew: עבד אל-עזיז א-זועבי‎; 4 February 1926 – 14 February 1974) was an Israeli Arab politician who served as a member of the Knesset for Mapam and the Alignment from 1965 until his death in 1974. When appointed Deputy Minister of Health on 24 May 1971, he became the first non-Jewish member of an Israeli government.
