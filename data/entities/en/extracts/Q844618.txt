Carlos Padrós Rubió, also known as Carles Padrós Rubió (9 November 1870, in Barcelona – 30 December 1950, in Madrid)  was a Spanish football pioneer and club President of Real Madrid.
He was a founding member of Real Madrid and later served as club President between 1904 and 1908, succeeding his brother Juan Padrós.Padrós was also the driving force behind the Copa del Rey and refereed the first ever final.
