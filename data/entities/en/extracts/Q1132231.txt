Little Women is a 1978 American television miniseries directed by David Lowell Rich and written by Suzanne Clauser based on the classic 1868 novel of the same name by Louisa May Alcott. The cast includes Susan Dey, Meredith Baxter Birney, Ann Dusenberry, Eve Plumb, Dorothy McGuire, William Schallert, Greer Garson, Robert Young, Richard Gilliland, William Shatner and John de Lancie.
