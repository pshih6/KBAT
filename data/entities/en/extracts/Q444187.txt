Cynthia Stevenson (born August 2, 1962) is an American actress. She first played a leading role in the syndicated parody comedy series My Talk Show (1990-91), before starring in a number of sitcoms, including Bob (CBS, 1992-93), Hope & Gloria (NBC, 1995-96), and Oh Baby (Lifetime, 1998-2000).
Stevenson made her big screen debut in the 1992 comedy film The Player. She later has appeared in a number of films including Forget Paris (1995), Home for the Holidays (1995), Happiness (1998), Air Bud: Golden Receiver (1998) and its sequels, and Agent Cody Banks (2003) and its sequel. Stevenson also starred in the Showtime comedy-drama Dead Like Me (2003-04) and ABC comedy-drama Men in Trees (2006-08).
