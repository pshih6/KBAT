Mieczysław Franciszek Rakowski ([mʲeˈt͡ʂɨswaf raˈkɔfskʲi] (listen); 1 December 1926 – 8 November 2008) was a Polish communist politician, historian and journalist who was Prime Minister of Poland from 1988 to 1989. He served as the seventh and final First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party from 1989 to 1990.
