Thomas Duane Arnold (born March 6, 1959) is an American actor and comedian.
He has appeared in several films, including Nine Months (1995), True Lies (1994), McHale's Navy (1997), Animal Factory (2000), Cradle 2 the Grave (2003), Mr. 3000 (2004), Happy Endings (2005), The Great Buck Howard (2008), and Madea's Witness Protection (2011). He was also the host of The Best Damn Sports Show Period for four years.
