Luciano Caldas Bivar (born 29 November 1944 in Recife) is a Brazilian politician and businessman who was the PSL candidate for the 2006 Brazilian presidential election. His political platform included projects such as the end of income tax in favour of a unique tax on products and services, and also building military barracks in Brazilian favelas. He received 0.06% of the total vote in the first round and did not progress to the second round.He was the president of Sport Club do Recife from 1989 to 1990, from 1997 to 2001, from 2005 to 2006 and in 2013.
