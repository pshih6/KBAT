Warda Al-Jazairia (Arabic: وردة الجزائرية‎  22 July 1939 – 17 May 2012) was an Algerian singer who was well known for her Egyptian Arabic songs and music. Her name literally meant Warda the Algerian, but she was sometimes referred to as just Warda (Egyptian Arabic: [ˈwæɾdæ] meaning "Rose") or as "The Algerian Rose" in the Arab world.
