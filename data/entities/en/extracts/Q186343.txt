Andrzej Józef Gut-Mostowy (born October 30, 1960) is a Polish politician. He was elected to the Sejm on September 25, 2005, getting 11282 votes in 14 Nowy Sącz district, candidating from the Civic Platform list. Reelected 2007, 2011 and 2015.From 2017 member of Agreement. In 2019 elected to the Sejm from the lists of Law and Justice.
