Kristian Kiehling (born 17 September 1976) is a German television, film and stage actor and filmmaker. In Germany he is known for Autobahnraser (2004), Tsunami (2005) and Verbotene Liebe (2013), in Britain for All the Small Things (2009) and Will (2011). In January 2014, Kiehling became a regular in the British soap opera, EastEnders, playing Aleks Shirovs, he left the show in 2015 due to a contractual dispute at the time it was due to be renewed. His directorial debut Chronicle of a Summer in Europe was premiered as the documentary centerpiece at the lighthouse international film festival 2016.
