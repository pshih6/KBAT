Nur Otan (Kazakh: Nur Otan) (lit. 'Radiant Fatherland'), is the ruling political party in Kazakhstan with over 762,000 members. The party was founded on 12 February 1999 after the merger of several previously independent pro-presidential parties, including the People's Union of Kazakhstan Unity, the Liberal Movement of Kazakhstan, and the "For Kazakhstan - 2030" Movement.
