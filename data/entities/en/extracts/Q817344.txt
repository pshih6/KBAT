Benito Cabrera Hernández (born 1982 in Lanzarote) is a Canarian timple composer and virtuoso.
He moved to Tenerife to study psychology in University of La Laguna.
Hernández is the author of the official hymn of the Autonomous Community of the Canary Islands. Benito Cabrera is the author of the song Nube de Hielo "Ice Cloud", one of the most deeply rooted songs between canaries.
