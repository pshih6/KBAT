He participated in 3 World Championship Formula One Grands Prix, debuting on July 21, 1962.  He scored no championship points. He also participated in numerous non-Championship Formula One races.
Chamberlain finished ninth overall at Le Mans in 1957, but first in class, driving a Lotus Eleven. Chamberlain of Burbank, California, was the United States distributor for Lotus cars.
