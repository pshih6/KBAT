Nuria González (born 16 May 1962) is a Spanish actress.
González was born in Málaga.  She is popular thanks to her appearances in several TV shows and series, but she has a long career as a film and stage actress ( 5mujeres.com  in Teatro Alcázar, Bodas de sangre, Cinco cubiertos, Fiestas gordas del vino y del tocino, Orgía, María Sarmiento...)
