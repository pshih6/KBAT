Wolfram Grandezka (born 17 December 1969 in Saalfeld, Thuringia) is a German actor.
He landed the role of Roman Klingenberg in the Daytime soap opera Unter uns. He played the role from 5 January 1998 to 26 April 2000. Wolfram came to another soap, Verbotene Liebe, four years later. He has played Ansgar von Lahnstein, a charming villain, since 22 January 2004.
From 1999 to 2005, he was married to model Nadja Auermann and has a son, Nicolas Robert (born 1999), with her.
