Ashokan is an Indian actor known for his roles in Malayalam films. He made his film debut with Peruvazhiyambalam (1979). He's best known for his roles in Yavanika (1982), Yuvajanotsavam (1986),Thoovanathumbikal (1987), Moonnam Pakkam (1988),In Harihar Nagar (1990), Amaram (1990), and 2 Harihar Nagar (2009).
