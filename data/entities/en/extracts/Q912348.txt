Brian Hodge is a prolific writer in a number of genres and subgenres, as well as an avid connoisseur of music.  He lives in Boulder, Colorado, where he is working on his latest novel.
Brian Hodge's novels are often dark in nature, containing themes such as self-sacrifice.  He often explores unique belief systems in his stories.He has been nominated for numerous awards, and won the International Horror Guild Award for best short fiction.
