Serafim Urechean (born 2 February 1950 in Larga village, Briceni rayon) is a Moldovan politician. He held the position of general mayor of Chișinău municipality (1994-2005) and interim prime minister of the Republic of Moldova (5 - 17 February 1999). He was the chairman of the party Our Moldova Alliance (2003 - 2011), first deputy chairman of the Parliament of the Republic of Moldova (2009 - 2010) and president of the Court of Accounts of the Republic of Moldova (2011 - 2016).
