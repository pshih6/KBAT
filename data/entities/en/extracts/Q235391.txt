María Isabel Verdú Rollán (born 2 October 1970), better known as Maribel Verdú (Spanish pronunciation: [maɾiˈβel βeɾˈðu]) is a Spanish actress. She played Luisa in Alfonso Cuarón's 2001 film Y tu mamá también and Mercedes in Guillermo del Toro's 2006 film Pan's Labyrinth. She has also appeared in Lovers (Amantes), Belle Époque, The Blind Sunflowers (Los girasoles ciegos) and Blancanieves.
