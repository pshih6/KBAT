Tokai University (東海大学, Tōkai Daigaku) is a private university founded by Dr. Shigeyoshi Matsumae with its headquarters in Tokyo, Japan. It was accredited under the Japan's old educational system in 1946 and under the new system in 1950. In 2008, Tokai University, Kyushu Tokai University, and Hokkaido Tokai University were consolidated and reorganized into Tokai University.
