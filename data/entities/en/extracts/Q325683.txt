Ksenia Mikhailovna Sitnik (Belarusian: Ксенія Міхайлаўна Сітнік, translit. Ksienija Michajlaŭna Sitnik; Russian: Ксения Михайловна Ситник, translit. Kseniya Mikhaylovna Sitnik; born 15 May 1995), sometimes also transliterated as Kseniya Sitnik or Xenia Sitnik, is a Belarusian singer. She represented Belarus in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2005, which she won with the self-penned song "My vmeste" (We Are Together).
