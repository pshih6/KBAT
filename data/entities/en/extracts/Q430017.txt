Oldřich Navrátil (born 21 October 1952) is Czech stage, film and television actor. He has appeared in 75 films and television shows since 1976 and starred in the 1982 film Incomplete Eclipse, which was entered into the 33rd Berlin International Film Festival.In recent years Navrátil has become a well-known, albeit reclusive, soap opera star. On 6 February 2018, Navrátil announced his candidacy for Senate at Třebíč district. He is a candidate of TOP 09.
