André-Joseph Léonard ((1940-05-06)6 May 1940) is a Belgian prelate who served as the  Archbishop of Mechelen-Brussels and Primate of Belgium after his installation on 27 February 2010. He had previously served as Bishop of Namur from 1991 until 2010 under the name André-Mutien Léonard. Pope Francis accepted his resignation on 1 June 2015.
