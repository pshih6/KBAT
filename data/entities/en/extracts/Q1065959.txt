Moonlight Lady (顔のない月, Kao no Nai Tsuki, lit. Faceless Moon) is the American release title for No Surface Moon The Animation; a 2001 Japanese OVA anime series adapted from the 2000 Japanese visual novel eroge of the same name. It was created by Orbit Co., Ltd., animated by IMAGIN, and produced by Pink Pineapple, and later licensed by Media Blasters, English dubbed by Wave Form Solutions (ep. 1-4), Sunlight Audio (ep. 5), and distributed by Kitty Media.
