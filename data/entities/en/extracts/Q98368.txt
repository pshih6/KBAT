Kurt Malangré (18 September 1934 – 4 October 2018) was a German politician from Aachen, belonging to the CDU.
Malangré started his political career in November 1969 when he became a member of Aachen's city council. In 1972 he was elected Mayor of Aachen, and from 1973–1989 he served as  Lord Mayor. From 1979 to 1999 he was also Member of the European Parliament.Malangré was a supernumerary member of Opus Dei. For his dedication for the good of the city and its citizens he was appointed honorary citizen of Aachen in 2004.
Malangré died on October 4, 2018 at the age of 84.
