Abdalá Jaime Bucaram Ortiz ( (listen) ahb-də-LAH buu-kə-RAHM; Arabic: عبد الله بوكرم‎; born February 20, 1952) is an Ecuadorian politician and lawyer who was President of Ecuador from August 10, 1996, to February 6, 1997.  As President, Abdalá Bucaram was nicknamed "El Loco Que Ama" ("The Madman That Loves," a nickname he himself championed) and was removed from office after being declared mentally unfit to rule by the National Congress of Ecuador. February 12, 1997. Bucaram and his followers claim that all cases against him have been dismissed. After having lived in Panama under political asylum, he returned to Ecuador in 2017 when the charges against him expired.
