Tomasz Kazimierz Tomczykiewicz (2 March 1961 – 28 November 2015) was a Polish politician. He was elected to the Sejm on September 25, 2005 getting 22,221 votes in 27 Bielsko-Biała district, candidating from the Civic Platform list and again in 2015 for the 8th Sejm.
He was also a member of Sejm 2001–2005, Sejm 2005–2007, Sejm 2007–2011. He was admitted to a hospital on 25 November 2015 due to a kidney ailment and died three days later.
