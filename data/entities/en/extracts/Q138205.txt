Micah Solusod (born August 21, 1990) is an American voice actor at Funimation. His best-known role in anime has been the title character Soul Evans in Soul Eater, which was broadcast on Adult Swim's programming block Toonami. He debuted as Malek Yildrim Werner in Blassreiter, and later went on to play Toma Kamijo in A Certain Magical Index, Yuichiro Hyakuya in Seraph of the End, and Yuri Plisetsky in Yuri on Ice.
