Grigory Anikeyev (born February 28, 1972 in Dutovo, Komi Republic) is a Russian politician and member of the State Duma of the Russian Federation.Anikeyev draw attention in April 2018 when he was named the member of State Duma of the Russian Federation with the highest income in 2017 - according to his personal tax declaration, his 2017 income amounted to RUB 4.3 BN (close to $70 MM) showing astonishing 717% growth from 2016
