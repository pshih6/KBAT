Robin Alan Thicke (born March 10, 1977) is an American-Canadian singer, songwriter, record producer and actor.He has collaborated with numerous artists, such as Nicki Minaj, 3T, T.I., Christina Aguilera, K. Michelle, Pharrell Williams, Usher, Jennifer Hudson, Flo Rida, Brandy, Kid Cudi and Mary J. Blige. Thicke worked on albums such as Usher's Confessions and Lil Wayne's Tha Carter III, while releasing his own R&B singles in the United States including "Lost Without U", "Magic", and "Sex Therapy". He gained international fame in 2013 with his single "Blurred Lines", which reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100. He is currently a judge on the Fox musical competition show The Masked Singer.
He is a son of the late Canadian actor Alan Thicke and actress Gloria Loring.
He married actress Paula Patton in 2005; in 2013 and 2014, their separation and divorce were covered extensively by the tabloid press.
