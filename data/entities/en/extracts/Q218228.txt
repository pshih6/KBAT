Zoe McLellan (born November 6, 1974) is an American television actress, known for her roles as Petty Officer Jennifer Coates in the procedural series JAG, as Lisa George in the comedy-drama soap Dirty Sexy Money, as Meredith Brody in the series NCIS: New Orleans (2014–2016) and as Kendra Daynes in season two (2017–2018) of the political drama Designated Survivor.
