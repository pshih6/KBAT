Temple City is a city in Los Angeles County, California. Temple City is part of a cluster of cities, along with Pasadena, Arcadia, Rosemead, Monterey Park, San Marino, and San Gabriel, in the west San Gabriel Valley with a rapidly growing Asian population. Temple City also has a Cuban and Puerto Rican community, among other Latino nationalities.  Approximately one third of the city's population is white. The population was 35,558 at the 2010 census.
