David Joshua Azrieli,  (Hebrew: דוד יהושע עזריאלי‎; May 10, 1922 – July 9, 2014) was an Israeli–Canadian real estate tycoon, developer,  designer, architect, and philanthropist. With an estimated net worth of $US 3.1 billion (as of March 2013), Azrieli was ranked by Forbes as the ninth wealthiest Canadian and 401st in the world.
