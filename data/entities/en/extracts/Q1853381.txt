Tewodros Kassahun Germamo (Amharic: ቴዎድሮስ ካሳሁን ገርማሞ; born July 14, 1976), known professionally as Teddy Afro, is an Ethiopian singer-songwriter. He is known for using his music to comment on the political climate of Africa, particularly his native Ethiopia.. His 2017 song "Ethiopia" has over 17 million views on YouTube.
