Pierre Karl Péladeau (born 16 October 1961), also known by his initials PKP, is a Canadian businessman, billionaire and former politician. He was also the MNA for Saint-Jérôme. Péladeau is the president and CEO of Quebecor Inc., Quebecor Media Inc. He used to own Sun Media Corporation. Péladeau is seen as a "strongly Quebec nationalist" and an influentional businessman in Quebec.Péladeau was the Leader of the Opposition in the Quebec National Assembly from his election as leader of the Parti Québécois on the 15 May 2015 until his resignation on the 2 May 2016 for family reasons. .
