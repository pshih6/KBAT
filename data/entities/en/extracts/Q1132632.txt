Corey Johnson (born May 17, 1961) is an American character actor largely active in the UK, best known for his supporting roles in Hellboy, Kingsman: The Secret Service, Captain Philips, The Bourne Ultimatum, Kick-Ass, Ex Machina, the Spooks episode "The Special" , the Doctor Who episode "Dalek" and The Last Days Of Lehman Brothers as Richard S. Fuld, Jr. , The Final Chairman & CEO of Lehman Brothers.
