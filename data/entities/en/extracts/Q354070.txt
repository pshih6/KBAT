Krzysztof Kowalewski (born 20 March 1937) is a Polish actor and comedian.
He was born to a Jewish mother, actress Elżbieta Herszaft-Kowalewska and Polish father, Cyprian Kowalewski, who was a soldier. 
His first wife Vivian came from Cuba. For many years he has been in a relationship with the actress Ewa Wiśniewska. They split up after Krzysztof met actress Agnieszka Suchora, whom he married in 2002. He has two children: Wiktor (from his relationship with Vivian) and Gabriela (with Agnieszka Suchora).
Kowalewski has been awarded the Knight's Cross of the Order of Polonia Restituta (2002), Gold Medal of Medal for Merit to Culture, "Gloria Artis" (2009) and Grand Prize Splendor (Wielki Splendor) (1992)
