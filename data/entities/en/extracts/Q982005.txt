Nicolas Jacques André Hulot (French pronunciation: ​[nikɔla ʒak ɑ̃dʁe ylo]; born 30 April 1955 in Lille, France; officier de la Légion d'honneur, chevalier des Arts et Lettres) is a French journalist and environmental activist. He is the founder and president of the Fondation Nicolas Hulot, an environmental group established in 1990.
Hulot ran as a candidate in the primary for the Europe Ecology – The Greens party in 2011 and eventually lost to Eva Joly. Hulot has refused to be a minister for Jacques Chirac, Nicolas Sarkozy and François Hollande.In May 2017, he was appointed Minister for the Ecological and Solidary Transition in the first Philippe government. On 28 August 2018, he announced his resignation from the government.
