Adam Jan Brodecki (1 August 1949 – 17 October 2010) was a Polish pediatrician, member of the Sejm (1989–1991), and competitive pair skater. With his skating partner, Grażyna Kostrzewińska (Osmańska), he placed 11th at the 1972 Winter Olympics in Sapporo.With his wife Halina, also a medical doctor, he had two sons, Marcin (born in 1971), Adam (1976), and a daughter, Katarzyna (1983).
