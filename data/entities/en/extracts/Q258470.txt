Crystal Marie Reed (born February 6, 1985) is an American actress. She is best known for her television roles as Allison Argent in Teen Wolf (2011–2014; 2016), Sofia Falcone in the fourth season of Gotham (2017–2018) and Abby Arcane in the series Swamp Thing (2019). Reed has also starred in the films Skyline (2010), Crush (2013) and Ghostland (2018).
