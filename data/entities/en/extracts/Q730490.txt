Robert Scott Weinrich (born September 29, 1961), known as Wino, is an American rock singer, guitarist and songwriter.
Active since 1976, when he started his first band War Horse, he is best known for being the frontman and guitarist for The Obsessed. He also performed for  Saint Vitus and other projects like The Hidden Hand, Spirit Caravan and Shrinebuilder. Weinrich is also a solo artist, having released three studio albums since 2009.
He has been highly influential in helping develop and codify doom metal's trademark sound, and is also considered an influential figure in the stoner rock and punk rock genres.
