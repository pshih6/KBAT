Johannes Hahn (born 2 December 1957) is an Austrian politician who has served as European Commissioner for Budget and Administration since 1 December 2019. 
He previously served as European Commissioner for European Neighbourhood Policy and Enlargement Negotiations from November 2014 to November 2019 and before that as European Commissioner for Regional Policy from 2010 to 2014. Before entering the European Commission, he served as the Austrian Minister for science and research from 2007 to 2010. He is a member of the Austrian People's Party, part of the European People's Party.
