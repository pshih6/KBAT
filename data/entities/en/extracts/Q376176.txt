Walter Marvin Koenig (; born September 14, 1936) is an American actor and screenwriter. His began acting professionally in the mid 1960s and quickly rose to prominence for his leading role as Ensign Pavel Chekov in Star Trek: The Original Series (1967-1969) second season. He went on to reprise this role in all seven subsequent Star Trek films. He has also starred in several other series and films including Goodbye, Raggedy Ann (1971), The Questor Tapes (1974), Babylon 5 (1993), and Crusade (1999).
In addition to his acting career, Koenig has made a career in writing as well and is known for working on Star Trek: The Animated Series (1973), Land of the Lost (1974), Family (1976), What Really Happened to the Class of '65? (1977) and The Powers of Matthew Star (1982).
