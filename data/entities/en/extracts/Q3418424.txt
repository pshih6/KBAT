Ramdane Touhami (born 23 September 1974 in Montauban, France) is a French-Moroccan artist, fashion designer, product designer, DJ, business man and journalist.Often called " a multi-disciplinary and polymath entrepreneur " by the international press, he has created different companies in the fashion, art, PR, and beauty industry since the early 90s.
Among the projects he has worked on, he counts with two ready-to-wear brands, " Résistance ", " RT " and " L’African", a restaurant based in Tangier, Morocco.
He is also known for revamping the world's oldest wax manufacture Cire Trudon.Alongside his wife Victoire de Taillac, he is behind the Parisian 19th-century beauty brand, l’Officine Universelle Buly.
