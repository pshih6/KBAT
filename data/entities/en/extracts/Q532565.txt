Nora Iuga (born 4 January 1931) is a Romanian poet, writer and translator.
Iuga was born in Bucharest, Romania on 4 January 1931. As well as being a writer, Iuga has also worked as a journalist, foreign language assistant, and editor.Her first collection of poems was published in 1968 and was called Vina nu e a mea (It Is Not My Fault).
