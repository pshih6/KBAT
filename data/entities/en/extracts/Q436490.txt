Tim Kincaid (born July 2, 1944) is an American  film director, film writer and film producer often credited as Joe Gage or Mac Larson.
Kincaid is well known for having directed several science fiction/horror films in the mid-eighties: Breeders, Mutant Hunt, and the now infamous Robot Holocaust, the latter featured in an episode of Mystery Science Theater 3000 and released on Blu-ray by DiabolikDVD.
