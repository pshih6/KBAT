Feldkirch (German pronunciation: [ˈfɛltkɪʁç]) is a medieval city in the western Austrian state of Vorarlberg on the border with Switzerland and Liechtenstein. It is the administrative center of the Feldkirch district. After Dornbirn, it is the second most populous town in Vorarlberg, slightly larger than the state capital of Bregenz. The westernmost point in Austria lies in Feldkirch on the river Rhine, at the northern tripoint between Austria, Switzerland, and Liechtenstein.
