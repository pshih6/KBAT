John Joseph Hall (born July 23, 1948) is an American musician, politician, environmentalist, and community activist. He was elected to the legislature of Ulster County, New York in 1989 and the Saugerties, New York Board of Education in 1991, and he was the U.S. Representative for New York's 19th congressional district, serving from 2007 to 2011. He is a member of the Democratic Party. Hall also founded the rock band Orleans in 1972 and continues to perform with them.
