Bo Johan Renck (born 5 December 1966 in Uppsala, Sweden) is a Swedish director of music videos, TV and film. He was an occasional singer-songwriter from 1991 to 2001, using the moniker Stakka Bo, and had an international hit with his single "Here We Go" in 1993. Renck later became a television and music video director, and won an Emmy Award in 2019 for his work on the mini-series Chernobyl.
