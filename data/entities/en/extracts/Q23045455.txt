XO (also known as XO Records) is a Canadian record label founded by singer The Weeknd and his manager Amir "Cash" Esmailian. As of 2012, it operates as a subsidiary of, and is distributed through, Universal Music Group's Republic Records. The label's current acts include The Weeknd, Belly, Nav, 88Glam, and Black Atlass. The label has had five projects debut at number one on the Billboard 200: Beauty Behind The Madness, Starboy, My Dear Melancholy, Bad Habits, and After Hours.
