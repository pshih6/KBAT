Neil Stephen Cicierega ( SISS-i-REE-g…ô; born August 23, 1986) is an American Internet artist, comedian, actor, filmmaker, singer, musician, puppeteer, and animator. He is the creator of the genre of Flash animation known as "Animutation", has released several albums as a musician under the name Lemon Demon and has released a series of mashup albums under his own name that have since gained a cult following.
