Krysten Alyce Ritter (born December 16, 1981) is an American actress, musician, author, and model. She is known for starring as the superhero Jessica Jones on the Netflix drama series Jessica Jones (2015–2019) and the miniseries The Defenders (2017). She has also portrayed Chloe on the ABC comedy series Don't Trust the B---- in Apartment 23 (2012–2013).
She also appeared as Jane Margolis on the AMC drama series Breaking Bad (2009–2010) as well its 2019 spin-off El Camino, a Netflix film. 
Ritter had roles in the series Veronica Mars, Gilmore Girls, 'Til Death, and Gravity. She has appeared in films such as What Happens in Vegas (2008), Confessions of a Shopaholic (2009), She's Out of My League (2010), Veronica Mars (2014), Big Eyes (2014), and The Hero (2017).
