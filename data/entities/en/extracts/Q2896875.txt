He was born in Rome, Italy on March 8, 1934 and died in Rome on November 30, 2010. He graduated from La Sapienza University in Rome and was a professor of commercial law at the Faculty of Law at La Sapienza University. He was president of TIM and Telecom Italia and vice-president of Unicredit.
