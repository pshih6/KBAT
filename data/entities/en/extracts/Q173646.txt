Mansour Akram Ojjeh (born 1952) (Arabic:منصور عجة) is a French Saudi Arabian-born entrepreneur who owns part of TAG, a Luxembourg-based holding company with interests worldwide. Ojjeh is the CEO of TAG, which owns 21% of the McLaren Technology Group, the most important asset of which is the McLaren Formula One team.
He is the former owner of TAG Heuer. He also owns 10% of the upmarket jewellers Asprey and Garrard.
