Lucy Clare Davis (born 17 February 1973) is an English actress who plays Hilda Spellman in the Netflix series The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina (2018–present). She is also known for her previous roles as Dawn Tinsley in the BBC comedy The Office (2001–2003), Dianne in the horror-comedy movie Shaun of the Dead (2004), and Etta Candy in Wonder Woman (2017).
