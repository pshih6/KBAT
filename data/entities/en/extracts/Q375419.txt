Corey Scott Feldman (born July 16, 1971) is an American actor, voice actor, singer, and activist. He became well known during the 1980s, with roles as a youth in films such as Gremlins (1984), The Goonies (1985), and Stand by Me (1986). In 1987, Feldman starred in the horror film The Lost Boys with Corey Haim; they became known as "The Two Coreys"  and went on to appear in other films together, including License to Drive (1988) and Dream a Little Dream (1989).
