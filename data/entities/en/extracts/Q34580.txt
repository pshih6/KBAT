Toshio Hayakawa (早川 俊夫, Hayakawa Toshio, born January 7, 1948), better known by his stage name Ichirou Mizuki (水木 一郎, Mizuki Ichirō), is a Japanese singer, lyricist, composer, voice actor and actor best known for his work on theme songs for anime and tokusatsu. For  over 40 years, he has recorded over 1200 songs for Japanese film, television, video and video games. He is referred to by fans and fellow performers alike as the Aniki (アニキ, "big brother") of the anison, or anime music genre. He has produced the singing duo Apple Pie since 1990 and created the Anison band JAM Project in 2000.
