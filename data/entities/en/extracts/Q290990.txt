Ryouta Murai (村井 良大, Murai Ryōta) is a Japanese actor best known for his roles as Kojirō (小次郎, Kojirō) in Fūma no Kojirō, Kazuya Suzuki (鈴木 一哉, Suzuki Kazuya) in Kamen Rider Den-O & Kiva: Climax Deka, Takuya Yano (矢野 拓也, Yano Takuya) in Osananajimi (幼なじみ) and Yusuke Onodera (小野寺 ユウスケ, Onodera Yūsuke) in Kamen Rider Decade.
