Lois Lowry (born Lois Ann Hammersberg on March 20, 1937) is a U.S. writer. She is the author of several books for children and young adults, including The Giver Quartet and Number the Stars. She is known for writing about difficult subject matters and complex themes in works for young audiences. 
Lowry has won two Newbery Medals: for Number the Stars in 1990 and The Giver in 1994.  Her book Gooney Bird Greene won the 2002 Rhode Island Children's Book Award.
