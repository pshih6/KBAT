Preslava Koleva Ivanova (Bulgarian: Преслава Колева Иванова)  (born Petya Koleva Ivanova) (Bulgarian: Петя Колева Иванова) on June 26, 1984), better known by her stage name Preslava, is a Bulgarian singer. She was born in Dobrich, Bulgaria. She is considered one of the key names in the Bulgarian modern music, and won more than 30 awards since her debut in 2004.
