Penn Dayton Badgley (born November 1, 1986) is an American actor and musician. He has received wide media attention, including for his roles as Dan Humphrey in The CW teen drama series Gossip Girl (2007–2012), and as Joe Goldberg in the Netflix thriller series You (2018–present). 
Badgley has appeared in a number of films, including
John Tucker Must Die (2006), The Stepfather (2009), Easy A (2010),  Margin Call (2011), and Greetings from Tim Buckley (2012). He is also the lead singer for Brooklyn-based indie band MOTHXR, whose studio album, Centerfold, was released in 2016.
