Olinka Hardiman (born 16 January 1960) is a French model and actress.
She is best known for looking like actress Marilyn Monroe and often portrayed her idol as a lookalike in the movies she appeared in, many of which were very adult in nature. She also did several adult magazine layouts. She was extremely supple and could perform contortions such as in Inside Marilyn (1985) where she performs the splits whilst standing on one leg, lifting the other leg up and pointing it at the ceiling.
