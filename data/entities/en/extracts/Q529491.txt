Anetta Kahane (born 1954 in East Berlin) is a German left-wing 
journalist, author and activist against antisemitism, racism and right-wing extremism. In 1998 she founded the Amadeu Antonio Foundation, which she leads since 2003. From 1974 to 1982 she had been an informant of the former East German Stasi.
This past has been used to discredit her and her work since 2002.
