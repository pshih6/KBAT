Jonathan Edward Bernthal (; born September 20, 1976) is an American actor. He is best known for his portrayal of Shane Walsh on the AMC horror series The Walking Dead (2010–2012; 2018) and Frank Castle / The Punisher on the Netflix action series Daredevil (2015–2018) and The Punisher (2017–2019). He began his career in theater before guest starring on various television shows. He has also starred in supporting and leading roles in films such as The Wolf of Wall Street (2013), Fury (2014), Sicario (2015), The Accountant (2016), Baby Driver (2017), Wind River (2017), Widows (2018), and Ford v Ferrari (2019).
