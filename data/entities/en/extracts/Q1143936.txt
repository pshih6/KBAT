Cuneiform Records is a record label in Silver Spring, Maryland.Founded in 1984, the label releases an mixture of musical styles, all with a Rock in Opposition aesthetic, including progressive jazz, jazz fusion, the Canterbury scene, and electronic music. Cuneiform has introduced many notable acts but also documents older bands who fit the profile, including its release of the Heldon catalog and several archival Soft Machine recordings. The label operates with a mail-order retailer, Wayside Music.Cuneiform announced that it would not release new music in 2018. Feigenbaum said he would assess the label's future.  In 2019 Cuneiform began releasing a few new albums again.
