Kazuhito Yamashita (山下和仁, born 1961 in Nagasaki) is a Japanese classical guitarist. His technique and expression are highly acclaimed. By the age of 32, Yamashita had already released 52 albums, including repertoires for solo guitar, guitar  concertos, chamber music and collaborations with other renowned musicians such as James Galway. To date, he has released a total of 83 albums.
