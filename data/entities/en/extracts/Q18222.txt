Andrea Joy Cook (born July 22, 1978), known professionally as A.J. Cook, is a Canadian actress. She is best known for her role as Supervisory Special Agent Jennifer "JJ" Jareau on the CBS crime drama Criminal Minds (2005-2020). She has also appeared in Higher Ground (2000), Ripper (2001), The Virgin Suicides (1999), Out Cold (2001), and Final Destination 2 (2003).
