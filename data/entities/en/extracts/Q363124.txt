Samuel Zell (born Shmuel Zielonka, September 28, 1941) is an American billionaire businessman and philanthropist. A former lawyer, Zell is the founder and chairman of Equity Group Investments, a private investment firm , founded 50 years ago, in 1968. The company invests opportunistically across industries and geographies and throughout the capital structure. He has substantial interests in, and is the chairman of, several public companies listed on the New York Stock Exchange: Equity Residential (EQR), Equity LifeStyle Properties (ELS),  Equity Commonwealth (EQC), Covanta Holding Corp. (CVA), and Anixter.
In 2018, Bloomberg ranked Zell as the world's 397th richest person, with a fortune of $4.4 billion.
