Rossiya Airlines OJSC (Russian: АО «Авиакомпания „Россия“», tr. AO «Aviakompaniya „Rossiya“»), sometimes branded as Rossiya—Russian Airlines (Russian: «Россия — Российские авиалинии», tr. «Rossiya — Rossyskiye avialinii»), is a Russian airline headquartered in Saint Petersburg with its hub at Pulkovo Airport with an additional hub at Vnukovo International Airport in Moscow. Rossiya is majority (75%) owned by Aeroflot.
