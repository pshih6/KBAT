Bohdan Osadchuk  (1 August 1920 – 19 October 2011) was a Ukrainian historian and journalist.Dr. Bohdan Osadchuk was born in Kolomyia.  He was a professor at the Free University of Berlin,  one of the most senior members of the Ukrainian Free University (UFU) of Munich, and a long-standing freelance writer for Kultura, a Polish emigre magazine published in Paris (editor Jerzy Giedroyc). In 2009 he was awarded a Bene Merito Honour medal by Foreign Minister Radosław Sikorski in recognition of scientific achievements and efforts to Polish-Ukrainian reconciliation.He died in Czechówka near Myślenice situated in the Lesser Poland Voivodeship.
