Piero Scaruffi (born 26 April 1955) is an Italian-American freelance software consultant and university lecturer who maintains a music website on which his reviews are published. Scaruffi also reviews many films. He has created his own publishing society called Omnipublishing that exclusively releases his books about music and science.Since 1983, Scaruffi has resided in Silicon Valley, California.
Scaruffi's website, which covers a wide range of topics, was the subject of a 2006 article by Dan Morrell in The New York Times.
