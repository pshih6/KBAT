Kavadarci (Macedonian: Кавадарци [kaˈvadartsi] (listen)) is a town in the Tikveš region of the Republic of North Macedonia. In the heart of North Macedonia's wine country, it is home to the largest winery in Southeast Europe, named after the Tikveš plain. The town of Kavadarci is the seat of Kavadarci Municipality. Situated near Kavadarci is North Macedonia's largest artificial lake, Lake Tikveš.
