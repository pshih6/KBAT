The canton of Zug (French: Zoug, Italian: Zugo, also English: canton of Zoug; German: Kanton German pronunciation: [tsuÀêk] (listen)) is one of the 26 cantons of Switzerland. It is located in central Switzerland and its capital is Zug. At 239 km2 (92 sq mi) the canton is one of the smallest of the Swiss cantons in terms of area. It is not subdivided into districts, but eleven municipalities.
