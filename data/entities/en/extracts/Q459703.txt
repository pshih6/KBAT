Nino Machaidze (Georgian: ნინო მაჩაიძე; born 8 March 1983) is a Georgian operatic soprano. She gained international attention after being cast as Juliette in Roméo et Juliette at the 2008 Salzburg Festival. She is also notable for bel canto roles such as Fiorilla in Il turco in Italia, Adina in L'elisir d'amore, and Violetta in La traviata.
