Georg Brunnhuber (born 18 February 1948 in Oberkochen) is a German politician of the Christian Democratic Union (CDU). As a member of parliament, he served on the committee for transport and the committee for construction and housing, and was deputy spokesman for transport of the CDU/CSU parliamentary group. From 2005, he was spokesman of the leaders of the CDU state groups of representatives, and a member of the executive committee of the CDU/CSU parliamentary group. In 2008, he made public that he would not stand for reelection in 2009. He was succeeded in 2009 by Roderich Kiesewetter.
Brunnhuber serves on the supervisory board of Deutsche Bahn.
Georg Brunnhuber is an engineer by profession. He is catholic, married and has two daughters.
