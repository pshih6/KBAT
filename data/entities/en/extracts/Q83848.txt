Carl Johan Rheborg (born 5 June 1963 in Täby, Sweden) is a Swedish comedian, actor and script writer. He is a member of the Swedish comedy group Killinggänget.
Rheborg is probably most known for his role as the space hero Kenny Starfighter, the irresponsible Percy Nilegård, and also as the talking stain in Tide's Super Bowl commercial. In 2015 he played Ove in the stage version of the book En man som heter Ove.
