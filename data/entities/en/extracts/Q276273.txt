Harry Jon Benjamin (born May 23, 1966) is an American actor, voice actor and comedian. He is known for his roles in various adult animated series such as Sterling Archer in Archer; Bob Belcher in Bob's Burgers; Carl Graves in Family Guy; Ben in Dr. Katz, Professional Therapist; Satan in Lucy, the Daughter of the Devil as well as Coach McGuirk and Jason Penopolis on Home Movies.
