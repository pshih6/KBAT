Anthony Marwood  is a British solo violinist.
He was educated at King Edward VI Grammar School in Chelmsford, at the Royal Academy of Music and the Guildhall School of Music and Drama.  His teachers included Emanuel Hurwitz and David Takeno.Marwood was artistic director of the Irish Chamber Orchestra from 2006 to 2011.  From 1995 to 2012, he was a member of the Florestan Trio with Susan Tomes and Richard Lester.
