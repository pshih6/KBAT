Mohammad-Rezā Lotfi (Persian: محمدرضا لطفی‎; also Romanized as Mohammad-Reza Lotfi; 1 January 1947 – 2 May 2014) was an Iranian classical musician renowned for his mastery of the tar and setar. He collaborated with singers such as Mohammad-Rezā Shajarian, Hengameh Akhavan , Shahram Nazeri and Alireza Shahmohammadi.
