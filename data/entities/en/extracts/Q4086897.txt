MSU Faculty of Biology  (Russian: Биологический факультет МГУ) is a faculty of Moscow State University, created in 1930. It is one of the largest faculties of MSU. There are about 1500 employees, including over 100 professors, 140 associate professors and teachers, 700 research associates. It prepares a wide range of shots on various biological specialties and it is the base for carrying out scientific researches on biology and adjacent areas. The Faculty Dean is - Mikhail Kirpichnikov, a member of the Russian Academy of Sciences.
