Ernst Dronke (born 1822, Germany - death 1891) was a journalist.  Because of his philosophical beliefs, Dronke became a "true socialist".  Later he became a member of the Communist League and became an editor of the Neue Rheinische Zeitung.  He participated in the German uprising of 1848-1849; after the suppression of this uprising, Dronke emigrated to England.  Subsequently, he withdrew from politics.
