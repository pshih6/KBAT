Le Figaro (French pronunciation: ​[lə fiɡaʁo]) is a French daily morning newspaper founded in 1826 and published in Paris. Le Figaro is the oldest national daily in France and is one of the three French newspapers of record, along with Le Monde and Libération.With its conservative, right editorial line, Le Figaro is the largest national newspaper in France, ahead of Le Parisien and Le Monde. In 2019, the paper had an average circulation of 321 116 copies per issue. The paper is published in the berliner format.
The newspaper has been owned by arms-producer Dassault Group since 2004 whose publications include TV Magazine and Evene.
