Pavol Rankov (born 1964) is a Slovak writer. He was born in Poprad and went to school in Bratislava. He studied library science at the Comenius University, graduating in 1987. He then worked at the Slovak National Library in Martin and at the Slovak Pedagogic Library in Bratislava. In 1993, he joined the staff of the Comenius University.
