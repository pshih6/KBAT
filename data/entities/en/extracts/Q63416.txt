Maria Gładkowska (born 16 September 1957 in Zielona Góra) is a Polish film, stage, and television actress. She gained critical acclaim in the 1980s that culminated in her Zbigniew Cybulski Award (1988). She portrayed Daisy, Princess of Pless, her breakthrough performance, in Magnat (1987) - which was included in the list of 100 Best Polish Films of all time.
