Kīrtanānanda Svāmī  (September 6, 1937 – October 24, 2011) was a Guru-Acarya of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness and the Founder-Acarya of New Vrindaban, a Hare Krishna community in Marshall County, West Virginia, where he served as spiritual leader from 1968 until he left it in 1994. He was also the first "sannyasin" of the ISKCON.
