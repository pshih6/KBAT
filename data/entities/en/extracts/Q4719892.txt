Alexander Putilov is a Czech Republic businessperson and co-owns Eurasia Drilling Company. Eurasia Drilling Company is provider of onshore drilling services in Russia and the Czech Republic. As of per Forbes list 2011, he is the 1,057th richest person in the world and 84th richest person in Russia. Putilov has net worth of US$1.1 billion. He is a first-generation entrepreneur and his wealth is self made.
