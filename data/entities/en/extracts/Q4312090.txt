Aliya Nursultanovna Nazarbaeva (Kazakh: Álıya Nursultanqyzy Nazarbaeva, 3 February 1980, Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic) is the youngest daughter of Nursultan Nazarbayev, the first President of Kazakhstan. She is the head of numerous business operations including a construction company and a high-end spa.Winner of the State Prize of the Republic of Kazakhstan in Literature and Arts (2018).
