Marsha Ambrosius-Billups (born 8 August 1977) is an English singer and songwriter from Liverpool, England. She embarked on her musical career as a member of Floetry. Ambrosius released her debut solo album Late Nights & Early Mornings in March 2011. Marsha's vocals were used in Justin Timberlake's "Cry Me a River" but was uncredited for her work. She also has a brother, Marvin Ambrosius, a television presenter in the United Kingdom.
