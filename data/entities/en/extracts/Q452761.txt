Giannina Braschi (born February 5, 1953) is a Puerto Rican writer based in New York City. Her notable tiles include  Empire of Dreams (1988)', Yo-Yo Boing! (1998), and United States of Banana (2011). 
Though categorized as novels, her cross-genre works are experimental and incorporate elements of poetry, drama, fiction, and political philosophy. Braschi writes in Spanish, Spanglish, and English. Her work explores the enculturation journey of Hispanic immigrants. Her work dramatizes the three political options of Puerto Rico: independence, colony, and state.
