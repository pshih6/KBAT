Ho Jong-suk (Korean: 허정숙; Hanja: 許貞淑; RR: Heo Jeong-suk; MR: Hŏ Chŏng-suk; July 16, 1908 – June 5, 1991) was a Korean independence activist, writer, journalist, and communist. Her real name was Jongja (정자; 貞子).She was a member of Singanhoe, Geunwoohoe and the Communist Party of Korea and also an early Korean women's rights activist.
