Iskandar Safa (born April 1955) is a French businessman of Lebanese origin., recently sued for $2B by Mozambique in a corruption scandal.  , 
Together with his brother Akram Safa, he is the owner of  Privinvest Holding, a major international naval construction group. In addition to this, Iskandar Safa and his brother Akram control, through P.I. Dev SAL, the French company FIMAS SA specialised in developing and managing real estate properties in the South of France.
