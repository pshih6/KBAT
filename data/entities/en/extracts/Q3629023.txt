EMI Records Limited (EMI Records Ltd.), also known as EMI United Kingdom between 1993 and 1998, was a British record label founded by the music company of the same name in 1972 as its flagship label, and launched in January 1973 as the successor to its Columbia and Parlophone record labels. The label was later launched worldwide. It has a branch in India called "EMI Records India", run by director Mohit Suri.
