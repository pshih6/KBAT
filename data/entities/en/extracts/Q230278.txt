Jennifer Audrey Coolidge (born August 28, 1961) is an American actress, comedian, and activist. She is best known for playing Jeanine Stifler in the American Pie franchise (1999–2012), Paulette Bonafonté Parcelle in the Legally Blonde franchise (2001–2003), and as Fiona in the romantic comedy A Cinderella Story (2004). Coolidge is also a regular actor in Christopher Guest's mockumentary films, such as Best in Show (2000), A Mighty Wind (2003), For Your Consideration (2006), and Mascots (2016).  
Coolidge has appeared in films, including Pootie Tang (2001), Click (2006), Date Movie (2006), Epic Movie (2007), American Dreamz (2006), Soul Men (2008), Gentlemen Broncos (2009), Austenland (2013), and Like a Boss (2020). On television, she is known for her starring role as Roberta "Bobbie" Morganstern in the NBC sitcom Joey (2004–2006), Betty in the ABC Family teen drama series The Secret Life of the American Teenager (2008–2012), Zofia ”Sophie” Kaczyński in the CBS sitcom 2 Broke Girls (2011–2017). In 2020, Coolidge appeared in Ariana Grande’s “Thank U, Next” music video. 
Coolidge is an alumna of The Groundlings, an improv and sketch comedy troupe based in Los Angeles.
