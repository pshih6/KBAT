Ken Rolston is an American computer game and role-playing game designer best known for his work with West End Games and on the computer game series The Elder Scrolls.  In February 2007, he elected to join the staff of computer games company Big Huge Games to create a new role-playing game.Rolston has a master's degree from NYU, and is a member of the Science Fiction Writers Association. He has been a professional games designer since 1982.
