Kanysh Imantayuli Satbayev (Kazakh: Қаныш Имантайұлы Сәтбаев, Qanysh Imantaıuli Sátbaev; Russian: Каны́ш Иманта́евич Сатпа́ев, Kanysh Imantaevich Satpaev) (April 11, 1899 – January 31, 1964) is one of the founders of Soviet metallogeny, principal advocate and the first president of Kazakhstan Academy of Sciences.
