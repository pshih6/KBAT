Christian Carstensen (born 11 March 1973, in Hamburg) is a German politician and member of the SPD. Carstensen was a member of the Hamburg-Nord district parliament (Bezirksversammlung) from 2001 to 2005. In 2005 he was elected to the Bundestag, the German federal parliament, representing Hamburg Nord, however he lost his seat at the 2009 election.
