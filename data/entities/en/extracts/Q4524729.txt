Sergey Nikolaevich Shishkarev (Russian: Серге́й Николаевич Шишкарёв, born on 2 February 1968 in Novorossiysk) is a Russian businessman and political figure. Founder (1993) and President of Delo Group, a transport and logistics holding company. President of the Russian Handball Federation. He served in the State Duma of the Russian Federation for three terms, from 1999 to 2011.
