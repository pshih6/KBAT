Detective Conan: Private Eye in the Distant Sea (名探偵コナン　絶海の探偵(プライベート・アイ), Meitantei Konan: Zekkai no Puraibēto Ai) is a 2013 Japanese animated drama thriller film part of the film series based on the Detective Conan manga and anime series. The movie aired on April 19, 2014 on Nippon TV as well. Its theme music is One More Time by Kazuyoshi Saito.
