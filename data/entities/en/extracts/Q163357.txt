Carlos Álvarez-Nóvoa Sánchez (1940 in La Felguera – 23 September 2015) was a Spanish theatre director, writer, actor and lecturer. He won the 2000 Goya award for Best New Actor for his performance as Vecino in Solas.In 2012 he played Tiberio Lucio Varo in Imperium. In 2015 she played Leonardo da Vinci in Carlos, rey emperador. He died on 23 September 2015 from lung cancer at the age of 75.
