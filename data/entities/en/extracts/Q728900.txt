Roland Duchâtelet (French: [dyʃɑtlɛ]; born 14 November 1946) is a Belgian businessman. He is the owner of football clubs: Carl Zeiss Jena and  Újpest 
He previously owned Standard Liege which he sold in early 2015, Sint-Truidense, which he sold in 2017, AD Alcorcón, which he sold in 2019 and Charlton Athletic, which he sold in 2020.
He is the founder of the social-liberal political party Vivant in Belgium.
