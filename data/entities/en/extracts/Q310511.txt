Armand Anthony Assante (; born October 4, 1949) is an American actor. He is best known for his roles as mobster John Gotti in the 1996 HBO television film  Gotti, Nietzsche in When Nietzsche Wept and Mickey Spillane's Mike Hammer in 1982's I, the Jury. His performance in Gotti earned him a Primetime Emmy Award and nominations for the Golden Globe Award and the Screen Actors Guild Award.
