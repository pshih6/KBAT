Livorno (Italian: [liˈvorno] (listen)) is a port city on the Ligurian Sea on the western coast of Tuscany, Italy. It is the capital of the Province of Livorno, having a population of 158,493 residents in  December 2017. It is traditionally known in English as Leghorn (pronounced  leg-ORN,  LEG-horn or  LEG-ərn).
