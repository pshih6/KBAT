Kruševo (Macedonian: Крушево, [ˈkruʃevo] (listen); Aromanian: Crushuva) is a town in North Macedonia. It is the highest town in North Macedonia, situated at an altitude of over 1350 m (4429 feet) above sea level. The town of Kruševo is the seat of Kruševo Municipality. The town is officially bilingual, Macedonian and Aromanian, hence both town names are official.
