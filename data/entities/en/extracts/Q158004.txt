Piotrków Trybunalski ([ˈpʲɔtrkuf trɨbuˈnalskʲi] (listen); also known by alternative names) German: Petrikau) is a city in central Poland with 73,670 inhabitants (2018). It is situated in the Łódź Voivodeship (since 1999), and was previously the capital of the Piotrków Voivodeship (1975–1998).  It is the capital of Piotrków County.
