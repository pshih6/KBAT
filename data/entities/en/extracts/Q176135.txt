Wedding Night in Paradise (German: Hochzeitsnacht im Paradies) is a 1950 West German musical comedy film directed by Géza von Bolváry and starring Johannes Heesters, Claude Farell, and Gretl Schörg.It is an operetta film, based on the 1942 stage work of the same title.
The film's sets were designed by Paul Markwitz and Fritz Maurischat.
