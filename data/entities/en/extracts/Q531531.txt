After the conclusion of the defeat in the parliamentary elections of 23 June 2013, Sokol Olldashi ran for the office of the chairman of the Democratic Party, after the resignation of its leader Sali Berisha.
He died on 20 November 2013 in a car accident in the outskirts of Tirana.He is survived by his wife Enkeleida and two sons, Glauk and Kleit.
