Dana Berger (Hebrew: דנה ברגר‎; born 2 November 1970) is an Israeli singer-songwriter and occasional actress. She is also a graduate of the Rimon School of Jazz and Contemporary Music. Berger came to fame in the 1990s, appearing on the popular TV show "Inyan Shel Zman". After becoming well known, she released a number of albums, which among them is the widely popular album "Ad Ha'Katze" (To the Edge), which was certified platinum and went on to sell over 60,000 copies in Israel.
