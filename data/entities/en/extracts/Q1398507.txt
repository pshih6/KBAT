He is best remembered for his voice which has been described as "honeyed" and "velvet-smooth".  One music journalist noted, "For years Junior Parker deserted downhome harmonica blues for uptown blues-soul music".
In 2001, he was inducted into the Blues Hall of Fame. Parker is also inducted into the Mississippi Musicians Hall of Fame.
