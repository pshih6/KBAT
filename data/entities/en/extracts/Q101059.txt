Eva Ingeborg Scholz (born 16 February 1928) is a German film and television actress. She made her debut in the title role of the 1948 film 1-2-3 Corona and appeared regularly in films over the following decade. From the early 1960s she appeared increasingly in television, where she is still active. In 2018, she won the Deutscher Schauspielpreis (German Actors Award) for her appearance in the Tatort episode Die Liebe, ein seltsames Spiel.
