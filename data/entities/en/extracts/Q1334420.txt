Ilya Zalmanovich Baskin Russian: Илья (Элиа) Залманович Баскин; born 11 August 1950), known as Elya Baskin, is a Russian–American actor. He first gained attention for his role in Moscow on the Hudson (1984), as Robin Williams' character's best friend. He is also known for playing Peter Parker's landlord, Mr. Ditkovich, in Spider-Man 2 and Spider-Man 3.
