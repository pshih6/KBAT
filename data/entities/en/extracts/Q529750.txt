She was killed when she accepted a ride from a drunk driver on December 15, 2001 in New Orleans. She was 36. Halstead is interred at Hollywood Forever Cemetery, Hollywood, California.
A substance abuse center called The Bianca Center for Substance Abuse, which opened in November 2008, is named in her honor.
