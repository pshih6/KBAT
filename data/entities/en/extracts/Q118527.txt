Wilhelm Amsinck Burchard-Motz (born 4 July 1878 in Hamburg, died 13 January 1963 in Hamburg) was a German lawyer and national-liberal politician. He served as Senator for Trade, Shipping and Industry of Hamburg from 1925 to 1933 and as Second Mayor from 1933 to 1934.  Burchard-Motz was a member of the Nazi Party.He was the son of Hamburg First Mayor Johann Heinrich Burchard and Emily Amsinck.
