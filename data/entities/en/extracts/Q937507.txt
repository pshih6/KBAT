Gleb Evgeniyevich Lozino-Lozinskiy (Russian: Глеб Евгеньевич Лозино-Лозинский), (Kiev, Russian Empire (now Ukraine), December 25, 1909 – Moscow, November 28, 2001) was a Russian and Ukrainian engineer, General Director and General Designer of the JSC NPO Molniya, lead developer of the Russian Spiral and Shuttle Buran programme, Doctor of Science, Hero of Socialist Labour, laureate of Lenin Prize (1962) and State Prizes (1950, 1952).
