Eliot Paulina Sumner (born 30 July 1990) is an English musician, actress, and electronic music producer. Sumner's debut album, The Constant (2010), was released under the band name I Blame Coco, while solo work has been released under the name Eliot Sumner. Sumner is the youngest daughter of musician Sting (born Gordon Matthew Thomas Sumner) and actress Trudie Styler.
