Griff Furst is an American actor and film director.Furst has appeared in over a hundred television and film productions. He has produced such films as You Might Be The Killer, Welcome to Sudden Death, and Tales From the Hood.  He directed Cold Moon.  He is the son of Lorraine (Wright) and actor Stephen Furst, and the brother of composer Nathan Furst.
His acting credits include the role of Jim Zien in the 2016 film The Founder, and Sheriff Lucas Hood in Banshee. Variety wrote of his acting in I Love You, Phillip Morris in the article entitled ‘Not Nominated, but Memorable’.
