Nawrahta Minye (Burmese: နော်ရထာ မင်းရဲ, pronounced [nɔ̀jətʰà mɪ́ɴ jɛ́]; also Anawrahta II of Sagaing) was king of Sagaing for seven months in 1349. He reversed his predecessor Kyaswa's policy of peace with Sagaing's cross-river rival Pinya although no war broke out. He was succeeded by his younger brother Tarabya II.
