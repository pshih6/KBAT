Dmitry Aleksandrovich Lukashenko (Russian: Дмитрий Александрович Лукашенко, Belarusian: Дзмітры Лукашэнка, Dzmitry Lukashenka; born 23 March 1980) is a Belarusian businessman and son of Alexander Lukashenko, the President of Belarus.
Dmitry Lukashenko graduated from the International Relations faculty of the Belarusian State University. He served in the Border Guard Service of Belarus.He is the head of the Presidential Sports Club.
