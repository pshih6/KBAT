Gros-de-Vaud District (French: District du Gros-de-Vaud) is a district in Vaud canton.
Gros-de-Vaud has an area, as of 2009, of 230.78 square kilometers (89.10 sq mi).  Of this area, 154.01 km2 (59.46 sq mi) or 66.7% is used for agricultural purposes, while 54.62 km2 (21.09 sq mi) or 23.7% is forested.  Of the rest of the land, 21.45 km2 (8.28 sq mi) or 9.3% is settled (buildings or roads) and 0.77 km2 (0.30 sq mi) or 0.3% is unproductive land.
