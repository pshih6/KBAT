Dietmar Bär (born 5 February 1961 in Dortmund) is a German actor. Since 1997 he has starred as Freddy Schenk in the popular television crime series Tatort since 1997 and in Ärzte (a TV Series in 1994). He has been in a few films including Männer by Doris Dörrie. He is a narrator of many audiobooks translated into German.
