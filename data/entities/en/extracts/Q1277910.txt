Sergej Trifunović (Serbian Cyrillic: Сергеј Трифуновић, pronounced [sɛ̌rɡɛːj trifǔːnɔʋitɕ]; born 2 September 1972) is a Serbian actor, comedian, singer, politician and citizen activist.In 2014, he founded a charity foundation Podrži život (Support Life), that helps underprivileged children with serious medical condition to get adequate treatment. He is the current president of Movement of Free Citizens.
