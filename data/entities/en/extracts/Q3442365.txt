Rose Bruford College of Theatre & Performance (formerly the Rose Bruford Training College of Speech and Drama) is a drama school in the south London suburb of Sidcup. The college has degree programmes in acting, actor musicianship, theatre arts and various disciplines of stagecraft.The college's undergraduate and postgraduate qualifications and programmes were validated by the University of Manchester, prior to the college gaining its own taught degree awarding powers in 2017. It is a member of the Federation of Drama Schools.
