María Guadalupe "Lupita" Jones Garay (Spanish pronunciation: [luˈpita ˈʝouns], [ʝons]; born 6 September 1967) is a Mexican actress, television producer, and beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Miss Universe 1991. She was the first Mexican woman to win the crown.
Since 1994, Jones has been the national director for Mexico at Miss Universe and is the franchise holder of Mexicana Universal.
