Nikos Vertis (born Nikolaos Vertis Arvanitidis; Greek: Νικόλαος Βέρτης Αρβανιτίδης; August 21, 1976) is a popular Greek singer. He was born in Gorinchem, Netherlands and his origin is from Galipsos, Kavala. To date, he has released five studio albums along with one CD single, and two special edition CD/DVD albums.
