The English Marriage (German: Die Englische Heirat) is a 1934 German comedy film directed by Reinhold Schünzel and starring Renate Müller, Anton Walbrook and Adele Sandrock. A young German woman is engaged to marry a British aristocrat but has to win over his hostile family. It was based on a novel by Ludwig von Wohl.
The film's sets were designed by the art director Otto Hunte.
