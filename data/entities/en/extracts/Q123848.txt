Edith Sitzmann (b. 4 January 1963, Regensburg) is a German politician and member of government of Baden-Württemberg since August 2002. She is a member of the state Landtag, representing the Freiburg II constituency and the Alliance 90/The Greens party. She is also the incumbent Minister of Finance for the state of Baden-Württemberg in the second cabinet of Minister-President Winfried Kretschmann. From 2011 to 2016, Sitzmann led the Green Parlimentary Group.
