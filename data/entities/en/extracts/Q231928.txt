Arielle Caroline Kebbel (born February 19, 1985) is an American actress and model. She has appeared in various television series including Gilmore Girls (2003-2004), The Vampire Diaries (2009-2017), Life Unexpected (2010), 90210 (2011-2013), Ballers (2015-2016, 2019), Midnight Texas (2017-2018) and Lincoln Rhyme: Hunt for the Bone Collector (2019-2020). Kebbel has also starred in films such as American Pie Presents: Band Camp (2005), John Tucker Must Die (2006), The Grudge 2 (2006), Forever Strong (2008), The Uninvited (2009) and Think Like A Man (2012).
