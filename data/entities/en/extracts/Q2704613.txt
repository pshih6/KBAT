Abu al-Hasan ash-Shadhili (Arabic: أبو الحسن الشاذلي‎) (full name: Abū al-Hasan ʿAlī ibn ʿAbd Allāh ibn ʿAbd al-Jabbār al-Hasanī wal-Husaynī ash-Shādhilī) also known as Sheikh al-Shadhili [593 AH/1196 AD – 656 AH/1258 AD] was an influential Moroccan Islamic scholar and Sufi, founder of the Shadhili Sufi order.
