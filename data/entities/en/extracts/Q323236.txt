Danny Quinn (born Daniele Anthony Quinn; 16 April 1964) is a Mexican-Italian actor. He is the son of Anthony Quinn. Since 1986 he has appeared in more than twenty films, including his role as Carlos in the cult film Band of the Hand.
From 1991 to 1993 he was married to the actress Lauren Holly.
Danny Quinns most famous movies are Maria Magdalena, Judas and Thomas, all from 2001. In all of the three movies he plays Jesus of Nazareth.
He received the America Award of the Italy-USA Foundation in 2019.
