Stanisław Gawłowski (born November 27, 1968 in Rozyna, near Brzeg) is a Polish politician. He was elected to the Sejm on September 25, 2005 getting 7879 votes in 40 Koszalin district, candidating from the Civic Platform list. He won election to the Senate in 2019 as an independent candidate. He has been accused of corruption.
