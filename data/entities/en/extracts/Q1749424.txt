Snizhne (Ukrainian: Сніжне; until 1864 — Vasylivka — Ukrainian: Василівка) is a significant regional city in eastern Ukraine within the Donetsk Oblast. Along with cities of Shakhtarsk and Torez, it is surrounded by Shakhtarsk Raion, according to the regional territorial division. The eastern edge of Snizhne is adjacent to administrative border of Luhansk Oblast. Its population is approximately 47,259 (2017 est.).
