Fingersmith is a two-part BBC mini-series that was televised in 2005.The story is an adaptation of Sarah Waters' novel of the same name and follows the meeting of two very different young women and what madness ensues. Directed by Aisling Walsh, it stars Sally Hawkins, Imelda Staunton, Elaine Cassidy, Rupert Evans and Charles Dance.The mini-series was nominated for Best Drama Serial at the 2006 British Academy Television Awards.
