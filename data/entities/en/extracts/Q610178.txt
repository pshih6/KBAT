Hideki Konno (紺野 秀樹, Konno Hideki) (born May 13, 1965) is a video game director and producer who works for Nintendo. He joined the company in 1986, realizing his dream after a long adoration of playing Famicom games. He was the lead producer for the Mario Kart series of games for years. In 2015, Konno began managing Nintendo's mobile projects at their Entertainment Planning & Development division.
