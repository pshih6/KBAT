Ahmed bin Abdulaziz Al Saud (Arabic: احمد بن عبد العزيز آل سعود‎; born 5 September 1942) is a member of House of Saud who served as deputy minister of interior of Saudi Arabia from 1975 to 2012 and briefly as Minister of Interior in 2012. He was detained in Saudi Arabia in March 2020 on the orders of his brother and nephew, King Salman and Crown Prince Mohammad, and charged with treason.
