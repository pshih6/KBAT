Sexy Voice and Robo (セクシーボイスアンドロボ, Sekushī Boisu Ando Robo) is a seinen manga by Iou Kuroda. It was originally serialized in the semi-alternative manga magazine Monthly Ikki between 2000 and 2003, and has also been published worldwide, including in North America by Viz Media and Spain by Ponent Mon. In 2007, it was adapted into a live action Japanese television drama, which aired every Tuesday on Nippon TV between April and June 2007, spanning a total of 11 episodes. The manga won A Grand Prize at the 2002 Japan Media Arts Festival, and has received critical acclaim around the world.Sexy Voice and Robo is currently unfinished. On his blog, Kuroda states that he intends to write more some day, but is too busy.
