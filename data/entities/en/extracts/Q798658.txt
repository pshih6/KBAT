Bad Boy is an
American record label founded in 1993 by Sean Combs. It operates as an imprint of Epic Records, a division of Sony Music. One of its most popular artists was The Notorious B.I.G. It has been home to many artists, including Craig Mack, French Montana, Mase, Machine Gun Kelly, 112, Total, The Lox, Danity Kane, Day 26, Elephant Man and many others.
