Irina Ivanovna Alfyorova PAR (Russian: Ири́на Ива́новна Алфёрова; born 13 March 1951, Novosibirsk, Soviet Union) is a Soviet and Russian actress. She was formerly married to Russian film and stage actor Aleksandr Abdulov, he adopted her daughter, Ksenia (from marriage to a Bulgarian diplomat Boyko Gyurov).
