Konotop (; Ukrainian: Конотоп [konoˈtɔp] (listen)) is a city in Sumy Oblast in northeastern Ukraine. Konotop serves as the administrative center of Konotop Raion. Konotop is administratively incorporated as a city of oblast significance and does not belong to the raion. Konotop is located about 129 km from Sumy, the oblast administrative center. It is host to Konotop air base. Population: 87,881 (2015 est.)
