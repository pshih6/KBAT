Igor Anatolyevich Komarov (born May 25, 1964, Engels, Saratov region) is a Russian industrialist, financier and manager. Plenipotentiary Representative of the President of the Russian Federation in the Volga Federal District from September 18, 2018 (acting from September 7 to September 18).
In March 2014, the government appointed the head of the United Rocket and Space Corporation (ORKK). Director General of the State Corporation for Space Activities "Roskosmos" (January 21, 2015 - May 24, 2018).
