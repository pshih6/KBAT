Identity Card (Czech: Občanský průkaz) is a 2010 Czech comedy film by Ondřej Trojan based on a story by Petr Šabach. The film premiered on 21 October 2010.The story, which takes place in Prague in the years 1974–77, tells about a group of fellow students: Peter, Cinderella, Ales and Mita. The film begins when Peter receives his identity card on his fifteenth birthday.
