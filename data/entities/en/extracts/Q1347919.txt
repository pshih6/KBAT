He also had solo hits on the R&B chart with "That's All Right" in 1950 and "Walking by Myself" in 1954. He withdrew from the music industry at the end of the 1950s but returned to recording and touring in the 1970s.
He is not to be confused with the country music singer Jimmie Rodgers or the pop singer Jimmie Rodgers.
