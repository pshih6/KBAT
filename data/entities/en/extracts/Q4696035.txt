Ahmed Sultan Bin Sulayem (Arabic: أحمد سلطان بن سليّم) is the Executive Chairman of DMCC (Dubai Multi Commodities Centre), the free zone authority in Jumeirah Lakes Towers (JLT) and established in 2002 by the Government of Dubai to provide the physical, market and financial infrastructure required to establish the emirate as a hub for global commodities trade. He is the son of Sultan Ahmed bin Sulayem. As of September 2018, DMCC had over 15,000 registered members operating within its free zone including commodities, businesses as well as other sectors. In 2018, Bin Sulayem completed 17 years with DMCC.
