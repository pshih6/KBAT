Zafer Şenocak (/zæˈfɑɹ ˈʃɛnoʊʤək/ born May 25, 1961)  is a German writer well known for his poems, essays, and books. He is often regarded as a speaker for Turks living in Germany and is a prominent voice in the discussion of multiculturalism and cultural identity in Germany. Born in Ankara, he has lived in Germany since 1970 and in Berlin since 1989.
