Raven Shamira Goodwin (born June 24, 1992) is an American actress. In television, she portrayed Tangie Cunningham on the comedy series Just Jordan, (2007–2008), Becca on the drama series Huge (2010), Ivy Wentz on the sitcom Good Luck Charlie (2010–2014), Niecy Patterson in the drama series Being Mary Jane (2013–2019), and Eliza on the comedy series SMILF (2017–2019). Her work in film includes starring as Annie Marks in the drama film Lovely & Amazing (2001), Cleo in the comedy film The Station Agent (2003), Young Jazmin Biltmore in the comedy film Phat Girlz (2006), and Lew in the action film Snatched (2017).
