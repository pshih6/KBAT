Rena Kato (加藤 玲奈, Katō Rena, born July 10, 1997) is a Japanese singer, dancer, model, and an actress. She is a member of the Japanese female idol group AKB48, and is in Team A.She was also a member, along with Anna Iriyama and Rina Kawaei, of a one-shot AKB48 subunit named Anrire. Formed in 2012, the trio released their first and only single "Ikujinashi Masquerade" with Rino Sashihara from HKT48, charting at number one in Japan.
