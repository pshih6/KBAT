Nicole de Boer is a Canadian actress.  She is best known for starring in the cult film Cube as Joan Leaven, playing Ezri Dax on the final season of Star Trek: Deep Space Nine (1998–1999), and as Sarah Bannerman on the series The Dead Zone (2002–2007). From 2016 to 2018, she had a recurring role as Becca Dorasay, ex-wife of one of the series leads on the Canadian-produced crime drama Private Eyes.
