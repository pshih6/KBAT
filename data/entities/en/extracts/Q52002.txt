Yanin "Jeeja" Vismitananda (Thai: ญาณิน "จีจ้า" วิสมิตะนันทน์; RTGS: Yanin "Chicha" Wisamittanan), née Nicharee Vismitananda (Thai: ณิชชารีย์ วิสมิตะนันทน์; RTGS: Nitchari Wisamittanan), (born March 31, 1984) is a Thai actress and martial artist. She specializes in Taekwondo. She is credited as Yanin Mitananda in Chocolate.
