Jonathan Michael Lovitz (; born July 21, 1957) is an American actor, comedian and singer. He is best known as a cast member of Saturday Night Live from 1985 to 1990. He starred as Jay Sherman in The Critic and has appeared in numerous other television series and films.
His film credits include roles in The Brave Little Toaster, Rat Race,  Big, A League of Their Own, The Wedding Singer, High School High, and The Benchwarmers.
