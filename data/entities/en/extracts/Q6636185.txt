Somdech Norodom Narindrapong(Khmer: នរោត្តម នរិន្រ្ទពង្ស) was a Prince of Cambodia. He was born in Phnom Penh on 18 September 1954 to King Father Norodom Sihanouk and Queen Mother Norodom Monineath Sihanouk. The prince studied philosophy, criminology, and law at the Moscow State University, and spoke fluent French and Russian. Prince Narindrapong died in his Paris apartment from a heart attack on 7 October 2003 at the age of 49. He was married and had two daughters:

Princess Norodom Simonarine (born August 1984)
Princess Norodom Moninouk (born March 1987)He was the younger full brother of King Norodom Sihamoni and half-brother to 12 other surviving siblings.
