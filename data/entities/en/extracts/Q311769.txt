Jason Bradford Priestley (born August 28, 1969) is a Canadian-American actor and director. He is best known as the virtuous Brandon Walsh on the television series Beverly Hills, 90210 (1990–2000), also his role starring as Richard "Fitz" Fitzpatrick in the show Call Me Fitz (2010–2013) and for his role starring as Matt Shade in the Canadian series Private Eyes (2016–present).
