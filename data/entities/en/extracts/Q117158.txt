Silvia Schenker (born January 17, 1954 in Aarau, the capital of the canton of Aargau, Switzerland) is a Swiss politician.
She joined the National Council of Switzerland (the lower house of the federal assembly) in 2003. Schenker is a member of the Commission for Social Security and Health (CSSS). As part of the Swiss Socialist Party she is vice-president and member of the steering committee.
