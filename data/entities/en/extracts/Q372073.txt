Cooper was the first child actor to receive an Oscar nomination. At age 9 he was the youngest performer to be nominated for an Academy Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role, an honor that he received for the film Skippy (1931).  For nearly 50 years, Cooper remained the youngest Oscar nominee in any category.
