He is well known for his distinct guitar work and drumming in the band Emperor, as well as his formation of the death metal band Zyklon. In very early Emperor releases, he was called Samot (his name "Tomas" backward), and with the formation of Zyklon he became known as Zamoth.  Samoth was the owner of the record label Nocturnal Art Productions and had a close relationship with Candlelight Records.
