"Rise of the Turtles" is the 2-part series premiere of the 2012 Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles animated series. Both parts aired on Nickelodeon on September 29, 2012.The Turtles rise to the surface for the first time since being mutated, and witness the Kraang, a race of brain-like aliens with robotic bodies, capturing April O'Neil and her father, Kirby. The Turtles attempt to rescue the O'Neils while learning that the strange green ooze possessed by the Kraang could hold many secrets relating to their mutations.
