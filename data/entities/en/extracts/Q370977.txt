Tripp Eisen (born Tod Rex Salvador; June 29, 1965), is an American musician and convicted sex offender, best known as the former guitarist of the industrial metal band Static-X. He is the guitarist for Face Without Fear and a former member of Dope, Murderdolls, Fractured Mirror (Ace Frehley tribute band), and Roughhouse  (formerly Teeze)Born in Pen Argyl, Pennsylvania, Eisen has made known his libertarian views to many fans throughout his years as a musician. He is a follower of Objectivism, the philosophy of Ayn Rand.
