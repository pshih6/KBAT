Sir Lindsay Owen-Jones, KBE, (born 17 March 1946), born in Wallasey, Merseyside to a Welsh family, was the Chairman and CEO of the cosmetics and beauty company L'Oréal from 1988 to 2011. 
In May 2019, he was ranked as the 292nd richest person in UK by The Sunday Times Rich List with a net worth of £467 million.
