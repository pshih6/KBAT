İstanbul Bilgi University (Turkish: İstanbul Bilgi Üniversitesi), officially established in 1996, is a foundation university located in İstanbul, Turkey.

The university has 4 campuses centrally-located in İstanbul namely santralistanbul, Kuştepe, Dolapdere and Kozyatağı.As of 2020, İstanbul Bilgi University has near 20,000 students and 45,000 graduates; approximately 1,500 academicians; 7 faculties, 3 institutes, 4 schools, 3 vocational schools, and more than 150 programs that provide education to its associate, undergraduate and graduate students.
