Nikolai Platonovich Patrushev (Russian: Никола́й Плато́нович Па́трушев, tr. Nikolay Platonovich Patrushev) (born 11 July 1951) is a Russian politician and security and intelligence officer. He served as Director of the Russian Federal Security Service (FSB), which is the main successor organization to the Soviet KGB (excluding foreign intelligence), from 1999 to 2008, and he has been Secretary of the Security Council of Russia since 2008.
