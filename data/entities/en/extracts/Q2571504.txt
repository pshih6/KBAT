Joseph Patrick "Joe" Escalante (born January 30, 1963) is an American  television writer, musician, radio host, film and music video director, and former television executive. He is most widely known as the bassist for the punk rock band The Vandals, and  former owner of their record label Kung Fu Records, and the host of Joe Escalante, Live from Hollywood, on the Los Angeles/Orange County radio station KEIB 1150AM Sundays 5-7PM PST.
