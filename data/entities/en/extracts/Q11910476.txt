James Greene (born 19 May 1931 in Belfast, Northern Ireland) is an Irish actor who has appeared in numerous plays and series on United Kingdom television over a 40-year period. He has often played lawyers, clergymen, army officers and latterly judges.Greene was a continuity announcer on Ulster Television from 1959 until 1965.
