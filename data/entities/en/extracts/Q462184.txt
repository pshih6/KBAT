Urbana  is a city in and the county seat of Champaign County, Illinois, United States. The population is estimated at 42,046 as of July 1, 2018. Urbana is the tenth-most populous city in Illinois outside of the Chicago metropolitan area. It is included in the Champaign–Urbana metropolitan area.
Urbana is notable for sharing the campus of the University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign with its sister city of Champaign.
