The Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México (Mexico Autonomous Institute of Technology), commonly known as ITAM, is a private Ph.D.-granting research university. It is one of Mexico’s most important institutions of higher learning; highly prestigious in the social sciences; regarded as the best undergraduate Economics, International Relations, Law, and Political Science school in Mexico; and the best Accounting and Business School in Latin America. Also, it is considered one of Mexico's think tanks and has the highest rank of admission to the Mexican Foreign Service.
