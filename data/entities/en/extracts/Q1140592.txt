Gregg Hoffman (June 11, 1963 â€“ December 4, 2005), born in Phoenix, Arizona, was a film producer responsible for developing Saw and Saw II.  He studied communications, law and economics at American University in Washington, D.C.  Hoffman was working on Saw III, Saw IV and other films for Twisted Pictures when he died in a hospital in Hollywood, California of natural causes.  He was 42 years old at his death. The movie Dead Silence (2007) was dedicated to him. He was also thanked in the film Gross Misconduct, mentioned as dedicatee for Saw III, and posthumously credited with producing the Saw films from 2007 through 2017.
