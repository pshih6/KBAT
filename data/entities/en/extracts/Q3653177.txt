Fadey Sargsyan (Armenian: Ֆադեյ Սարգսյան, September 18, 1923 – January 11, 2010) was an Armenian scientist and politician.
Sargsyan served as the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Armenian Soviet Socialist Republic from 1977 to 1989.He was later the President of the Armenian National Academy of Sciences from 1993 to 2006.
