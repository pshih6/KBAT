Elena Velevska (Macedonian: Елена Велевска; born October 26, 1980) is a Macedonian turbo folk and popular music singer. Due to her dress and musical style, which resembles that of Svetlana Ražnatović, as well as her strong presence in the yellow press of the successor states of Yugoslavia, she is at times called "the Macedonian Ceca". In 2007, she conducted a world tour, playing in front of large diaspora audiences.
In 2008, it was announced that she is expecting a baby and therefore is having a break from the music. She also married in beginning of the year in Ohrid.
