Jason White (born November 11, 1973) is an American musician and singer, best known as the touring rhythm guitarist for the American punk rock band Green Day. He has played as a touring member of Green Day since 1997. In 2012, White became a full member of Green Day and also participated in the recording sessions for their ¡Uno!, ¡Dos!, ¡Tré!  album trilogy. However, his tenure as a full-time member ended in 2016. He is also the guitarist/vocalist for the Californian punk band Pinhead Gunpowder, and co-founder of Adeline Records alongside Billie Joe Armstrong, and guitarist and lead vocalist for the Green Day side project The Coverups.
In late 2014, he was diagnosed with tonsil cancer, which has since been treated.
