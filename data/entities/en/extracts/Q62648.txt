Antje Traue (pronounced [ˈʔantjə ˈtʀaʊ̯ə]; born 18 January 1981) is a German actress. She speaks German and English fluently, and appeared in her first English-language role in the film Pandorum. She may be internationally best known for her portrayal of the villainous, indestructible character of Faora in the 2013 Superman movie Man of Steel, and her role as Agnes Nielsen in the German Netflix series Dark.
