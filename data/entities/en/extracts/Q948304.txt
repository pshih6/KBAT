Pszczyna [ˈpʂt͡ʂɨna] (English: Pless, German: Pleß, Czech: Pština, formerly: Blština or Blščina, Silesian: Pszczyna) is a town in southern Poland with 25,823 inhabitants (2019) within the immediate gmina. The town is in the Silesian Voivodeship. It was a part of Katowice Voivodeship from 1975 until administrative reform in 1998.
