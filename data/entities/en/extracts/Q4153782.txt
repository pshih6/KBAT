Mikhail Ivanovich Davydov (Russian: Михаил Иванович Давыдов; born October 11, 1947) is a Russian doctor and medical scientist. He has received the Russian State Prize Laureate, is an Honored Science Worker of the Russian Federation, Director of the Russian Cancer Research Center, member of the Presidium and Academician of RAS, member and President of the Russian Academy of Medical Sciences.
