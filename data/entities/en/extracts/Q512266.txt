Thomas Gautier Pesquet (French pronunciation: ​[tɔma gotje pɛskɛ]; born 27 February 1978 in Rouen) is a French aerospace engineer, pilot, and European Space Agency astronaut. Pesquet was selected by ESA as a candidate in May 2009, and he successfully completed his basic training in November 2010. From November 2016 to June 2017, Pesquet was part of Expedition 50 and Expedition 51 as a flight engineer. Pesquet will return to space in late 2021 on board a US Commercial Crew Vehicle for a second six-month stay on the ISS.
