Scott Samuel "Scooter" Braun (born June 18, 1981) is an American entrepreneur, philanthropist, and record executive. Braun is also co-founder of the comic-film studio Mythos Studios, along with Hollywood producer David Maisel, and co-owner of the esports team 100 Thieves.In 2013, Braun was included on the annual Time 100 list of the most influential people in the world. In 2018, Braun co-organized March for Our Lives, the student-led demonstration for stricter gun laws which USA Today measured as the largest single-day protest in Washington, D.C. history.
