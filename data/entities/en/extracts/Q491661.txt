Guano Apes are a rock band formed in 1994 in Göttingen, Germany. Since its formation the band has consisted of Sandra Nasić (vocals), Henning Rümenapp (guitars, backing vocals), Stefan Ude (bass, backing vocals) and Dennis Poschwatta (drums, backing vocals).AllMusic calls the quartet alt-rock and their sound a fusion of metal, pop and rap.Over the course of its career the band has released five studio albums, one live album, two compilation albums, five video albums and fifteen singles.
