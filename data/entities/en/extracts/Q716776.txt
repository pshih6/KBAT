Leo Huberman (October 17, 1903 in Newark, New Jersey – November 9, 1968) was an American socialist economist. In 1949 he founded and co-edited Monthly Review with Paul Sweezy.  He was the chair of the Department of Social Science at New College, Columbia University; labor editor of the newspaper PM;  and the author of the popular history books Man’s Worldly Goods and We, The People.
