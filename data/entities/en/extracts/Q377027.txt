Oleg Ivanovich Betin (Russian: Олег Иванович Бетин) (born August 25, 1950) is a governor of Tambov Oblast in Russia. He was a member of Our Home – Russia party.  In 1995 he became governor. He was re-elected on December 7, 2003 with around 70% of the votes cast. Under Betin, Tambov Oblast provided food shipments to war-torn Chechnya in 2000.
