Revalina Sayuthi Temat (born 26 November 1985) is an Indonesian actress. She was born in Jakarta, Indonesia. The daughter of Sayuti Temat, who is of Malay-Palembang descent, and Rachmaniar, who is of Minangkabau descent. She became popular after starring the soap opera Bawang Merah Bawang Putih in 2004.
