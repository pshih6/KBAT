Katerina Tikhonova (Russian: Катери́на Ти́хонова, IPA: [kətʲɪˈrʲinə ˈtʲixənəvə], née Yekaterina Vladimirovna Putina, Екатери́на Влади́мировна Пу́тина; born 31 August 1986) is a Russian acrobatic dancer, and director of two initiatives at Moscow State University: the National Intellectual Development Foundation (NIDF) and the National Intellectual Reserve Centre (NIRC). She is the daughter of Russian President Vladimir Putin.
