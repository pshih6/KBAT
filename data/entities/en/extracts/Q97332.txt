Barbara Anne Hendricks (born 29 April 1952) is a German politician and member of the SPD party.
From 2013 until 2018, Hendrick served as Federal Minister for the Environment, Nature Conservation and Nuclear Safety. From 2007 to 2013, she was Federal Treasurer of the SPD, and from 1998 to 2007 she was Parliamentary Secretary of State at the Federal Ministry of Finance.
