Shiri Maimon (Hebrew: שירי מימון‎; born 17 May 1981) is an Israeli pop/R&B singer, TV show host and actress, who rose to fame as the runner-up in the TV show Kokhav Nolad. She represented Israel in the Eurovision Song Contest 2005, where she came fourth.
In 2008 Maimon was nominated and performed in the MTV Europe Music Awards. In June 2013 Maimon became one of the judges for the first season of The X Factor Israel. She continued her role for the second and third seasons. On 2018 It was revealed that Maimon would leave the show in favor of the rival network show, Rising Star Israel.
In 2018 Maimon started starring in the Broadway show "Chicago" in the role of Roxie Hart.
