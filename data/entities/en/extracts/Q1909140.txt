Matt Bayles (born October 19, 1972) is a record producer, mixer, engineer, and musician based in Seattle, Washington. Bayles (pronounced Bay-Less) is known for his work with bands such as Isis, Soundgarden, Pearl Jam, Botch, Mastodon, Minus the Bear, Burnt by the Sun, and Norma Jean and as the keyboardist for American indie rock band, Minus the Bear.
