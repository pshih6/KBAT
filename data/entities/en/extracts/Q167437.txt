The Jackal is a 1997 American political action thriller film directed by Michael Caton-Jones, and starring Bruce Willis, Richard Gere, and Sidney Poitier. The film involves the hunt for a paid assassin. It is a remake of the 1973 British-French film The Day of the Jackal, which starred Edward Fox. The film earned mostly negative reviews from critics, but was successful at the box office, grossing $159.3 million worldwide against a $60 million budget.
