Haute-Ajoie is a municipality in the district of Porrentruy in the canton of Jura  in Switzerland. It was founded on January 1, 2009 by the former municipalities of Chevenez, Damvant, Réclère and Roche-d'Or.On 1 January 2018 the former municipality of Rocourt merged into the municipality of Haute-Ajoie.
