Dichen Lachman(; born 22 February 1982) is an Australian actress. She appeared in the soap opera Neighbours as Katya Kinski and in Joss Whedon's science fiction drama television series Dollhouse as Sierra. Lachman portrayed Suren in the American remake of Being Human as a main character during the show's second season. Lachman has also starred in the 2014 film Lust for Love and had a recurring role in Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.. Lachman stars as Reileen Kawahara in Netflix's Altered Carbon. She has also had a regular role in the TV show Animal Kingdom.
