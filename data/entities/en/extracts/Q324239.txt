Walking and Talking is a 1996 American independent film written and directed by Nicole Holofcener and starring Catherine Keener, Anne Heche, Todd Field, Liev Schreiber and Kevin Corrigan.
The film is #47 on Entertainment Weekly's "Top 50 Cult Films of All-Time" list.The film's soundtrack includes music from Billy Bragg, Yo La Tengo, Liz Phair, and The Sea and Cake.
The film is notable for being the first collaboration between Holofcener and Keener and international co-production between France, Germany, the United Kingdom and the United States.
