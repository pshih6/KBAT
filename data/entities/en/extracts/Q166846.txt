Yerzhan Khozeuly Kazykhanov (Kazakh: Erjan Hozeuly Qazyhanov, [jɛrˈʒɑn xɔˌzɛ.uˈlə qɑzəˈxɑnəf], born 21 August 1964) is the ambassador of Kazakhstan to the United States. Kazykhanov presented his credentials to President Trump on April 24, 2017. Prior, he was Kazakhstan's Ambassador to the UK and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Kazakhstan from April 2011 to September 2012.Kazykhanov is a career diplomat and served as Kazakhstan's Permanent Representative to the United Nations from 2003 to 2007. He is married. The result of it are two children.
