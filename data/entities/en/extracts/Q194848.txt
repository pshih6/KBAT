Margareta Pogonat (full last name Pogonat-Caliopi; March 6, 1933 – May 11, 2014) was a Romanian actress, participating in theatre and film.Pogonat studied at the Institute of Theatre and Cinematographic Art in Bucharest, graduating in 1959. She made her debut as a student in 1957, and then acted in theaters in Botoşani, Iaşi and Ploieşti. In 2009 she received a Lifetime Achievement Award from the Romanian Association of Theatre Artists.
