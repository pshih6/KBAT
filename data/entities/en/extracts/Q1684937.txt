Verzasca is a proposed municipality in the district of Locarno in the canton of Ticino in Switzerland.
The new municipality will be created on 5 April 2021 by the merger of Corippo, Vogorno, the village of Lavertezzo Valle which constitutes the major part of Lavertezzo, Brione, Gerra Valle of the municipality of Cugnasco-Gerra, Frasco and Sonogno. The case of the Lavertezzo Piano exclave was not yet settled in December 2019, but was dropped from the final proposed municipality in March 2020.
