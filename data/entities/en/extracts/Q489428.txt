DSP Media (Hangul: DSP 미디어), is a South Korean entertainment company established in 1991 by Lee Ho-yeon. The company operates as a record label, talent agency, music production company, event management and concert production company, and music publishing house.
The label is home to prominent artists such as Oh Jong-hyuk, APRIL, Heo Young-ji, KARD and formerly to pioneering groups SoBangCha, ZAM, COCO, Fin.K.L and Sechskies as well as SS501, KARA, Rainbow and A-JAX. It also manages actors such as Lee Hyun-joo and Cho Shi-yoon. It's also home to former X1 member, Son Dong Pyo.
