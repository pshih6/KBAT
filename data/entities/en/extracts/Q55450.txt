Aldo Fabrizi (born Aldo Fabbrizi; Italian pronunciation: [ˈaldo faˈbrittsi]; 1 November 1905 – 2 April 1990) was an Italian actor, director, screenwriter and comedian, best known in United Kingdom for the role of the heroic priest in Roberto Rossellini's Rome, Open City and as partner of Totò in a number of successful comedies.
