Mosley Music Group (MMG) is a record label founded and formed by producer Tim "Timbaland" Mosley. Founded in 2006, the imprint was formerly distributed exclusively by Interscope Geffen A&M until 2014 when Timbaland made a new joint venture with L.A. Reid at Jive Records which is under Sony Music Entertainment.
The label's roster includes OneRepublic, Nelly Furtado, Keri Hilson, Ian Grey and Timbaland himself.
