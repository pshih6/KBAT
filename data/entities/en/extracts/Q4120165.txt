The Perelman School of Medicine, commonly known as Penn Med, is the medical school of the University of Pennsylvania. It is located in the University City section of Philadelphia. Founded in 1765, the Perelman School of Medicine is the oldest medical school in the United States and is one of the seven  Ivy League medical schools. Penn Med is consistently one of the top recipients of NIH research awards and is currently ranked third for research among American medical schools by U.S. News & World Report.
