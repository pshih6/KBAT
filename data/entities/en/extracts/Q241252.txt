Alexei Fyodorovich Lvov (Russian: Алексей Фёдорович Львов) (June 5, 1799 – December 28, 1870) was a Russian composer. He composed the imperial Russian national anthem Bozhe, tsarya khrani (also known as God Save the Tsar). He wrote the opera Undine in 1846. He was entombed in the Pažaislis Monastery, Kaunas (Lithuania).
