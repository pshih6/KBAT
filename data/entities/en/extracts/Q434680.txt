Nava Semel (Hebrew: נאוה סמל‎; born Nava Artzi; 15 September 1954 – 2 December 2017) was an Israeli author, playwright, screenwriter and translator. Her short story collection Kova Zekhukhit  (Hat of Glass) was the first work of fiction published in Israel to address the topic of the "Second Generation" - children of Holocaust survivors.
