Urassaya Sperbund (Thai: อุรัสยา เสปอร์บันด์; born March 18, 1993), more commonly known as Yaya, is a half Thai half Norwegian actress and model known for her roles in Duang Jai Akkanee (2010), Game Rai Game Rak (2011), Torranee Ni Nee Krai Krong (2012), Kleun Cheewit (2017) and The Crown Princess (2018).
