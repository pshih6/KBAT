Alikhan Nurmukhamedovich Bukeikhanov (Kazakh: Әлихан Нұрмұхамедұлы Бөкейханов, Álıhan Nurmuhameduly Bókeıhanov; Russian: Алихан Нурмухамедович Букейханов; March 5, 1866 – September 27, 1937) was a Kazakh statesman, politician, publicist, teacher, writer and environmental scientist who was a founder and led the Alash party. He served as the Prime Minister of the Alash Autonomy from 1917 to 1920, making him the first person in the history of Kazakhstan to hold such a position.
