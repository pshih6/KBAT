School of Language and History – Geography (Turkish: Dil ve Tarih – Coğrafya Fakültesi, abbreviated DTCF) is a school of the Ankara University, Turkey. It doesn't have a common campus with the Ankara University. DTCF has its own building on Atatürk Boulevard of Ankara at 39°55′47″N 32°51′20″E. The architect of the building was Bruno Taut. The school was founded in 1940 before the establishment of Ankara University. In 1946 it was included in the newly established Ankara University.
