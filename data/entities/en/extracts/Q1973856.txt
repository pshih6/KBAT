Irina Bogushevskaya (Russian: Ири́на Алекса́ндровна Богуше́вская, IPA: [ɪˈrʲinə ɐlʲɪˈksandrəvnə bəɡʊˈʂɛfskəjə] (listen); born 2 November 1965) is a Russian singer, poet, and composer of theater jazz and cabaret rock. Her largest event was a one-woman concert at the Kremlin Palace in 2005, which drew 6,000 people.
