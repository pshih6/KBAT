The district of Östlich Raron (fr. District de Rarogne oriental) is an administrative district in the canton of Valais, Switzerland.  The districts of Östlich Raron and Westlich Raron share a single Community Identification Number of SFOS number (2309).  The district of Östlich Raron has a population of  
3,003, while the Raron District has a population of 10,940 (as of 31 December 2018).
