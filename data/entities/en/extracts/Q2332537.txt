Visitor from the Future (Russian: Гостья из будущего, Gostya iz budushchego lit Guest from the future) is a five-part Soviet children's science fiction television miniseries, made at Gorky Film Studio, first aired in 1985. It is based on the novel One Hundred Years Ahead (Russian: Сто лет тому вперёд, Sto let tomu vperod) by Kir Bulychov.The series starred Natalya Guseva as Alisa Selezneva, a girl from the future that travels to the present and Aleksei Fomkin as Kolya Gerasimov, a boy, who lives in the year 1984 and travels to the future in the year 2084.
The series was highly popular in the Soviet Union. It is periodically rerun .
