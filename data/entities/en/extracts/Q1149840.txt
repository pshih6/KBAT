Michel Ray David (ミシェル・レイ・デビッド, Misheru Rei Debiddo, born September 6, 1988), better known under his stage name Rei Fujita (藤田 玲, Fujita Rei), is a Japanese actor and musician from Tokyo. His mother is Japanese and his father is French. His most notable roles to date are as Kitazaki/Dragon Orphnoch in the 2003 series Kamen Rider 555 and Rei Suzumura/ZERO the Silver Fanged Knight in the 2006 series GARO. He is also the lead vocalist of the band Dustz.
