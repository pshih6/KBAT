Christopher Maltman is a British operatic baritone.
Maltman grew up in Lincolnshire, attending King Edward VI Grammar School, Louth, and studied music at the Royal Academy of Music. In 1994 he won the Great Elm Festival Vocal Award (now the Maureen Lehane Vocal Awards).  During The Royal Opera 2013/14 season, he played both Count Almaviva in Le nozze di Figaro and Lescaut in Puccini's Manon Lescaut.
