Jasmin Schwiers (born 11 August 1982 in Eupen, Belgium) is a German actress.
Schwiers grew up in the German-speaking community in Eastern Belgium. She started her career with appearances in several German TV series and TV movies. She also acted in German movies like Schule (2000) (together with Daniel Brühl) and NVA (2005).
Schwiers has received the newcomer prize Günter-Strack-Fernsehpreis (2000) and the award in the newcomer category of the Goldene Kamera (2005).
