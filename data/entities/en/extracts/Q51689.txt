Plano ( PLAY-noh) is a city in the U.S. state of Texas, located approximately 20 miles (32.2 km) north of downtown Dallas. The city of Plano is a part of the Dallas-Fort Worth metropolitan area. Plano lies mostly within Collin County, but includes a small portion that extends into Denton County.
The city is a hub for many corporate headquarters. Plano was also considered to be the safest city in the nation by Forbes in 2011.
