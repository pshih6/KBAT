Radboud University  (abbreviated as RU, Dutch: Radboud Universiteit, formerly Radboud Universiteit Nijmegen and before that Katholieke Universiteit Nijmegen) is a state funded Roman Catholic university with a strong focus on research located in Nijmegen, the Netherlands.  RU has been included in the top 150 of universities in the world by four major university ranking tables.
RU has seven faculties and enrolls over 22,000 students. The university features many student associations which encourage participation in extracurricular activities.
