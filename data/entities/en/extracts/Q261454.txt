Cynthia Gibb (born December 14, 1963) is an American actress and former model who has starred in film and on television. She began her career starring in the musical drama series Fame (1983-87) and later has appeared in films Youngblood (1986), Salvador (1986), Malone (1987), Short Circuit 2 (1989) and Death Warrant (1990). She also received Golden Globe Award nomination for her performance as Gypsy Rose Lee in Gypsy (1993).
