Sébastien Tellier (French: [tɛlje]; born 22 February 1975) is a French musician, singer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist. He is mainly known for having represented France in Eurovision Song Contest 2008 with his song Divine. He has also produced songs for Dita Von Teese and he has composed music for the French films Narco and Steak among others. He is currently signed to Record Makers, a French independent record label. He sings in many languages as English, French and Italian.
