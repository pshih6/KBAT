Michel Robin (born 13 October 1930) is a French film actor and comedian. He has appeared in 120 films since 1966.He has worked with directors including William Klein, Costa-Gavras, Marcel Camus, Gérard Oury, Andrzej Żuławski, Jean L'Hôte, Francis Veber, José Giovanni, Claude Chabrol, Alexandre Astruc, Jim Henson, Jaco Van Dormael, Édouard Molinaro, Pascal Thomas, Jean-Paul Salomé, Jean-Pierre Mocky, Jean-Pierre Jeunet, Sylvain Chomet, Benoît Jacquot, Alain Resnais and Arnaud Desplechin.
