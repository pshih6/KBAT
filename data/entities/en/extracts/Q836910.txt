Vincențiu Babeș also known in (Hungarian: Babes Vince, Hodoni village, Satchinez Commune, Timiș County - 21 January 1821 - 22 January 1907, Budapest) was an ethnic Romanian  lawyer, teacher, journalist and politician from the Banat, settled in Vienna, where he married Sophia Goldschneider in 1851.
He was one of the founding members of the Romanian Academy. He was the father of Victor Babeș.
