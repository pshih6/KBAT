Yiyun Li (Chinese: 李翊雲; born November 4, 1972) is a Chinese-American writer. Her short stories and novels have won several awards and distinctions, including the PEN/Hemingway Award and Guardian First Book Award for A Thousand Years of Good Prayers, 
and the 2020 PEN/Jean Stein Book Award for Where Reasons End.
She is an editor of the Brooklyn-based literary magazine A Public Space.
