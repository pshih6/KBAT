Willigis Jäger (German: ['vɪlɪgɪs 'jɛ:gər]; 7 March 1925 – 20 March 2020) was a German Benedictine friar, mystic, and Zen master who trained and taught in the Sanbo Kyodan tradition. He founded a centre of Zen and contemplation at the Münsterschwarzach Abbey in 1983, and his own Benediktushof, an inter-faith centre of meditation and awareness, in 2003.
