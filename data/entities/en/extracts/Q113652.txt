Richard Frey (11 February 1920 in Vienna – 16 November 2004 in Beijing), also known by his Chinese name Fu Lai (Chinese: 傅莱), was a Chinese doctor and politician originally from Austria. He fled due to the Second World War from Austria to China and spent his entire life in his adopted country. Because of his outstanding contributions to national independence, national liberation and to build China's land, he has gained a high reputation in China.
