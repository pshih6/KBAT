Vikram Shankar Pandit (born 14 January 1957) is an Indian American banker and investor who was the chief executive officer of Citigroup from December 2007 to 16 October 2012 and is the current chairman and chief executive officer of The Orogen Group.Pandit has been honoured with the Padma Bhushan third highest Indian civilian award for his contributions to the trade and industry in 2008 by the Government of India.
