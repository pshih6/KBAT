Włocławek [vwɔt͡sˈwavɛk] (listen) (German: Leslau) is a city located in central Poland along the Vistula (Wisła) River and is bordered by the Gostynin-Włocławek Landscape Park. As of December 2018, the population of the city is 110,802. Located in the Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, it was the capital of Włocławek Voivodeship until 1999.
The city is located in the historical region of Kuyavia and is the region's second largest city after Bydgoszcz.
