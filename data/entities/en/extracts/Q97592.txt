Stefan Waggershausen (born 20 February 1949) is a German singer, composer, and songwriter.
Waggershausen was born in Friedrichshafen, at Lake Constance. In 1974 he produced his first record as singer. In 1980, he had his first big success with the song Hallo Engel.
