Drama Centre London (often abbreviated as Drama Centre) is a British drama school in King's Cross, London, where it recently moved after a major reshaping of the university. The school is part of Central Saint Martins, a constituent college of the University of the Arts London. In March 2020 UAL announced that the Drama Centre would close when the current students had completed their courses.
It is a member of the Federation of Drama Schools.The school offers BA (Hons) and MA acting courses.
