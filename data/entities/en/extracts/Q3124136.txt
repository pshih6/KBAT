Gérard C. Louis-Dreyfus (June 21, 1932 – September 16, 2016), also known as William, was an American businessman.  His net worth was estimated at $3.4 billion by Forbes in 2006. He was the chairman of Louis Dreyfus Energy Services and the great grandson of Léopold Louis-Dreyfus, founder of Louis Dreyfus Group. He was the father of actress Julia Louis-Dreyfus.
