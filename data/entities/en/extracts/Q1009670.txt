Bunny is a 1998 American computer-animated short film by Chris Wedge and produced by Blue Sky Studios.
It was featured on the original DVD release of Ice Age from 2002 and its 2006 "Super Cool Edition" re-release to coincide with the release of Robots, which was also directed by Chris Wedge.
Influenced by the classic Uncle Wiggily illustrations by Lansing Campbell, the short features the music of Tom Waits.
Bunny won the Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film in 1998  as well a Golden Nica at the Prix Ars Electronica.
