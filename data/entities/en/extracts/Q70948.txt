Hansjörg Felmy (31 January 1931 – 24 August 2007) was a German actor.He appeared in 50 films and television shows between 1957 and 1995. He starred in the film The Marriage of Mr. Mississippi (1961), which was entered into the 11th Berlin International Film Festival. In the German television crime series Tatort he played police investigator Heinz Haferkamp from 1974 to 1980, who remains well known in Germany.
In his last years he suffered from osteoporosis. He died in Eching near Munich. Felmy was the son of the German airforce general and war criminal Hellmuth Felmy (1885-1965).
