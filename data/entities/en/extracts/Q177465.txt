Raivis Dzintars (born 25 November 1982 in Riga) is a Latvian right-wing politician. He is the co-chairman of the National Alliance with Gaidis Bērziņš.He was elected to the Saeima at the 2010 parliamentary election.  He was the Alliance's candidate for Prime Minister at the 2011 election, at which the party increased its number of seats from 8 to 14.
