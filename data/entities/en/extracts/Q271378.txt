Špela Pretnar (born 5 March 1973 in Bled, SFR Yugoslavia) is a former alpine skier.
In her career, Pretnar won six races in Alpine Skiing World Cup races, with 13 podiums altogether. In the 1999–2000 season, she won a small crystal globe in slalom. Pretnar was named Slovenian sportswoman of the year in 2000. Pretnar represented Slovenia at the 2002 Winter Olympics.Currently, Pretnar is a sports reporter at a Slovenian commercial TV station POP TV.
