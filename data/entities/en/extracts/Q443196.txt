Xia Meng (simplified Chinese: 夏梦; traditional Chinese: 夏夢; pinyin: Xià Mèng; 16 February 1933 – 30 October 2016), a.k.a. Hsia Moon and Miranda Yang, born Yang Meng (Chinese: 楊濛; pinyin: Yáng Méng), was a Hong Kong actress and film producer.  She was a key figure of Hong Kong's Left Wing film scene.
Xia Meng's younger sister Yang Jie (杨洁) played on the China women's national basketball team from 1954 to 1957.
