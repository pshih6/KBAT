Kenneth Anderson (born March 6, 1976) is an American professional wrestler, professional wrestling trainer, and ring announcer, who is currently signed to National Wrestling Alliance as a wrestler and with Top Rank Boxing as their announcer. Anderson is known for his tenure with WWE from 2005â€“2009 under the ring name Mr. Kennedy. With WWE, Anderson is a former one-time WWE United States Champion and won the 2007 Money in the Bank contract at WrestleMania 23.
In 2010, Anderson signed with Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (now Impact Wrestling), where he competed under the ring name Mr. Anderson and is a two-time World Heavyweight Champion. He departed TNA in 2016 and opened a wrestling school called The Academy in Minneapolis, Minnesota later that year, along with Shawn Daivari, Molly Holly, Ariya Daivari, and Arik Cannon.
Before working for WWE, Anderson wrestled for numerous promotions, winning various singles and tag team championships. He has also worked occasionally as an actor and starred as Master Chief Petty Officer Carter Holt in the WWE Studios production Behind Enemy Lines: Colombia in 2009.
