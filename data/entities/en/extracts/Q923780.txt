San Juan Capistrano (; Spanish: [saŋ ˈxwaŋ kapisˈtɾano]; Spanish for '"Saint John of Capestrano"') is a city in Orange County, California, United States. The current OMB metropolitan designation for San Juan Capistrano and the Orange County area is Santa Ana–Anaheim–Irvine, CA. The population was 34,593 at the 2010 census, up from 33,826 at the 2000 census.
