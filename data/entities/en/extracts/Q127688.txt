Johan Vilhelm Snellman (Swedish pronunciation: [ˈjǔːhɑn ˈvǐlːhelm ˈsnělːmɑn] (listen); 12 May 1806, Stockholm – 4 July 1881, Kirkkonummi) was an influential Fennoman philosopher and Finnish statesman, ennobled in 1866. He was one of the most important national awakeners alongside Elias Lönnrot and J. L. Runeberg.
