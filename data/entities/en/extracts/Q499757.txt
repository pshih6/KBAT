Tarakany! (previously Chetyre Tarakana "The 4 Cockroaches") is a Russian (Soviet) punk rock band based in Moscow. Tarakany! is one of the most famous Russian punk bands to have achieved mainstream success. Formed in 1991, the band has released 14 studio albums, 5 live albums, as well as a lot of splits and compilations. Tarakany! has repeatedly toured Russia and other CIS countries, Europe, Japan, Israel, Georgia, and Kazakhstan.
Tarakany! is one of the few Russian rock bands to create original music in both Russian and English.
