Boris Yurievich Titov (Russian: Борис Юрьевич Титов; born 24 December 1960) is a Russian politician, businessman, and Presidential Commissioner for Entrepreneurs’ Rights. He has led the Party of Growth (Russian: Партия Роста; Partiya Rosta, pronounced [ˈpartʲɪjə ˈrostə]) previously known as "Just Cause" (Russian: Правое Дело, romanized: Právoye Dyelo, lit. 'Just Cause' pronounced [ˈpravəjə ˈdʲɛɫə]) since February 2016.In 2018 election Titov run for President of Russia, but was defeated by incumbent Vladimir Putin.
