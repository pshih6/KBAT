Kaukab Noorani Okarvi (Urdu: کوکب  نورانی  اوکاڑوی‎ born 17 August 1957) is an Islamic scholar, researcher, orator, writer and cleric of Ahle-Sunnat Wa Jamaa’at Sunni. He is known as Khateeb-e-Millat ("Orator of the Nation") by his followers. He is the son of Khateeb-e-A’zam Muhammad Shafee Okarvi, who was founder of the Jamaa'at-e-Ahle-Sunnat, main Sunni Barelvi organisation of Pakistan.
