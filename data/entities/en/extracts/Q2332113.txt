The Adventures of Paddington Bear is a Canadian-French animated children's television series. It is based on the book Paddington Bear by Michael Bond and was written by Bruce Robb. It was produced by CINAR and Protecrea and ran for 117 episodes.
The show aired in the United States on the Cookie Jar Toons block on This TV from November 2008 to August 2009. It was also shown on HBO, as of 2017, it now airs on Starz Kids and Family and on Light TV. In 2018, Qubo Channel announced they would start airing reruns of the show beginning October 7th. In 2019, Nick Jr. announced that a remake that is in development. It premiered on January 20th, 2020.
