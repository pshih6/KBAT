Hermann Otto Solms (born Hermann Otto Prinz zu Solms-Hohensolms-Lich, on 24 November 1940) is a German politician of the Free Democratic Party (FDP).
Between 1980 and 2013 he has been member of the Bundestag, from 1991 to 1998 as Chairman of the FDP parliamentary party and from 1998 to 2013 as Vice President of the Bundestag. Since 2017 he has again been member of the Bundestag.
