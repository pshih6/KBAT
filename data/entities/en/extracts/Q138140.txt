Deniz Akdeniz (born May 16, 1990) is an Australian actor. He was nominated for the "Award for Best Actor" at the 2010 Inside Film Awards, for his role of "Homer" in the Australian box office hit Tomorrow, When the War Began. Akdeniz is also known for his role of "Raff" in the Disney television production As the Bell Rings, and for portraying Aladdin on Season 6 of the ABC television series Once Upon a Time.
