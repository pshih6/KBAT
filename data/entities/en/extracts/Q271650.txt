María Cristina Rota Fernández (La Plata, 1945) is an Argentine-Spanish actress, producer and drama teacher settled in Spain.
With actor Diego Fernando Botto she had actor Juan Diego and actress María Botto, she also had another daughter, the actress Nur Al Levi, with another partner. 
Many famous Spanish actors studied interpretation in her school: Malena and Ernesto Alterio, Guillermo Toledo, Alberto San Juan, Nathalie Poza, Andrés Lima, Luis Bermejo, Penélope Cruz, Fernando Tejero, Roberto Álamo, Eduardo Noriega, and others.
