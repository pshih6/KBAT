Grodno, or Hrodna (Belarusian: Гродна [ˈɣrɔdna]; Russian: Гродно, tr. Grodno, IPA: [ˈɡrodnə]; Polish: Grodno; Yiddish: גראָדנע‎, romanized: Grodne, see also other names) is a city in western Belarus. It is located on the Neman close to the borders of Poland and Lithuania (about 15 km (9 mi) and 30 km (19 mi) away respectively). It has 373,547 inhabitants (2019 census). It is the capital of Grodno Region and Grodno District.
