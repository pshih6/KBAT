Palmetto Records is an independent American jazz record company and label in New York City founded in 1990 by guitarist Matt Balitsaris.Issues began with those by Balitsaris, then Greg Hatza in 1993. Eventually it added to its catalogue Peter Bernstein, Joel Frahm, Larry Goldings, Andrew Hill, Cecil McBee, Dewey Redman, and Matt Wilson.Balitsaris retired from music and began working for Fonkoze, a charity in Haiti. He was chairman of the board until 2015.
