Max Azria (Hebrew: מקס עזריה, Arabic: ماكس عزرية‎; January 1, 1949 – May 6, 2019) was a Tunisia-born fashion designer who founded the contemporary women's clothing brand BCBGMAXAZRIA. Azria was also the designer, chairman and CEO of the BCBG Max Azria Group, a global fashion house that encompassed over 20 brands. Azria left BCBG in 2016. BCBG Max Azria filed for bankruptcy in 2017 and was sold to Marquee Brands and Global Brands Group. 
Later Azria served as the CEO of ZappLight.
