A motivational speaker or inspirational speaker is a speaker who makes speeches intended to motivate or inspire an audience. Such speakers may attempt to  challenge or  transform their audiences. The speech itself is popularly known as a pep talk.
Motivational Speakers can deliver speeches at schools, churches, motivational companies, corporations, government agencies, conferences, trade shows, summits, community organizations and similar environments.Motivational speaker makes an impact on the community by influencing their pattern of thinking.
