'Ilya Filippovich Mikhalchuk (Russian: Илья Филиппович Михальчук), born 2 January 1957 in Kuybyshev, is a former Russian politician, Head of the East-Siberian Construction Company representing the interests of the SU-155 Group of companies in Siberia.From 2008 to 2012, Governor of Arkhangelsk Oblast; from 1998 to 2007, Mayor of Yakutsk.
