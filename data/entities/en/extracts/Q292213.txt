Gabrielle Beaumont (born 7 April 1942 in London, England) is a British film and television director. 
Her directing credits range from Hill Street Blues to Star Trek: The Next Generation. She is best known for directing, writing and producing the TV special Diana: A Tribute to the People's Princess. She and writer/actor Olaf Pooley both contributed to a film version of Bernard Taylor's The Godsend. Pooley wrote the screenplay for the film while Beaumont directed it.
