Niklas Zennström (Swedish pronunciation: [ˈnɪ̌kːlas ˈsɛ̂nːstrœm] (listen); born 16 February 1966) is a Swedish billionaire entrepreneur known for founding several high-profile online ventures with Janus Friis including Skype and Kazaa. He also founded the technology  investment firm Atomico and has become a significant figurehead for entrepreneurs in the tech sector. Zennström is also the co-founder of the charity organization Zennström Philanthropies.
