Anna Maria Francesca Perez de Tagle (born December 23, 1990) is an American actress and singer. She is known for her roles as Ashley Dewitt on Hannah Montana and Ella Pador on Camp Rock and Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam. She also played Miracle Ross on Cake and was in the 2009 film Fame, in which she starred as Joy Moy.
She also went on tour with the Jonas Brothers and Demi Lovato (Jonas Brothers Live in Concert).
In November 2011, she began starring on Broadway in the Godspell revival at the Circle in the Square theater in New York City.
