Alfons Freiherr von Czibulka, or Alfons Cibulka (born 28 June 1888, Ratboř Castle (Schloss Radborsch) near Kolín, Bohemia – died 22 October 1969, Munich) was a Czech-Austrian writer and painter. (Pseudonym A. von Birnitz)Czibulka was the son of general Freiherr Hubert von Czibulka and Marie von Birnitz. In 1919 he co-founded the magazine 
Der Orchideengarten with Karl Hans Strobl, a fantasy magazine which also published some science fiction
and  detective stories.In the Third Reich he received the "Literary Prize of the City Munich" in 1938.
