Shawnee Rebecca Smith (born July 3, 1969) is an American actor and singer. She is known for her portrayal of Amanda Young in the Saw franchise and for starring as Linda in the CBS sitcom Becker (1998–2004). She co-starred as Jennifer Goodson, the ex-wife of Charlie Goodson (Charlie Sheen), on the FX sitcom Anger Management (2012–2014).
In addition to acting, Smith once fronted the rock band Fydolla Ho, with which she toured globally. Later, with actor Missi Pyle, she served as half of Smith & Pyle, a country rock band.
