Gérard Haché (March 17, 1925 – September 22, 2017) was a Canadian politician. He served in the Legislative Assembly of New Brunswick from 1967 to 1970 as member of the Liberal party.Haché co-found farm equipment dealer G.G. Haché & Frères Ltée in 1947, was a councilor in Bathurst, New Brunswick and served as school trustee.
