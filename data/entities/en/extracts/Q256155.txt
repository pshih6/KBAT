Devan (born 5 May 1954) is an Indian film actor, television actor and politician who is known for his work in Malayalam, Tamil and Telugu language films.After doing a lot of the second hero or supporting actor roles he advanced to a lead actor with his films like Oru Minnaminunginte Nurunguvettam (1987), Oozham (1988), Simon Peter Ninakku Vendi (1988) and Aranyakam (1988). His well-known movies such as Vietnam Colony (1992), Ekalavyan (1993), Honest Raj (1994), Baashha (1995) and Indraprasdham (1996).
