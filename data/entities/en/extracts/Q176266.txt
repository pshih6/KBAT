Anushavan Danielyan (Armenian: Անուշավան Դանիելյան; born 1 August 1954) was Prime Minister of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic from June 1999 through September 2007. He was succeeded by Arayik Harutyunyan. Danielyan was a vice-speaker of the Supreme Council of Crimea for the party Party of Economic Revival of Crimea in the 1990s.
