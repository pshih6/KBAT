Ivan L. Moody (born Ivan Lewis Greening on January 7, 1975) is the lead vocalist for American heavy metal band Five Finger Death Punch. He had performed for several bands before joining Five Finger Death Punch (often abbreviated to FFDP or 5FDP). As an actor, he also starred in the films Bled as Incubus, and The Devil's Carnival as the hobo clown. He was also known by the pseudonym Ghost during his time with Motograter.
