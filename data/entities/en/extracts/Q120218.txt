Pierre Imhalsy (14 November 1939 – 17 June 2017) was a Swiss novelist and poet.Imhasly was born in Visp, Switzerland. He studied German literature in Fribourg and Zurich. His major work is The Rhone Saga an epic poem about the Rhône River. Imhasly was the principal translator of Maurice Chappaz's works. Imhasly died 17 June 2017 from cancer.
